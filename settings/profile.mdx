---
title: Profile settings
description: Configure your personal profile and preferences
---

Manage your personal information, display preferences, and account settings from the Profile settings page.

## Accessing profile settings

1. Click the **Settings** icon in the sidebar
2. Select **Profile** from the menu

## Personal information

### Basic details

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Profile picture** | Upload an avatar (PNG, JPEG, GIF, max 10MB) |
| **First name** | Your first name |
| **Last name** | Your last name |
| **Email** | Your account email (read-only) |

### Professional details

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Job role** | Your position or title |
| **Company** | Your organization name |
| **Phone** | Phone number with country code |
| **Country** | Your country of residence |
| **LinkedIn** | Your LinkedIn profile URL |

## Time and locale preferences

Configure how dates and times display throughout the app:

### Timezone

Select your local timezone from the searchable dropdown. All scheduled events and timestamps will display in your selected timezone.

### Time format

| Option | Example |
|--------|----------|
| 12-hour | 3:30 PM |
| 24-hour | 15:30 |

### Date format

| Format | Example |
|--------|----------|
| MM/DD/YYYY | 03/15/2026 |
| DD/MM/YYYY | 15/03/2026 |
| YYYY-MM-DD | 2026-03-15 |

### Week start

Choose when your week begins:

- Sunday
- Monday

This affects calendar views and weekly reports.

## Language settings

Select your preferred display language:

- English
- Français (French)

The interface, notifications, and system messages will display in your selected language.

## Saving changes

Profile changes save automatically when you move to a different field. A confirmation message appears briefly to indicate the save was successful.

<Tip>
  Keep your profile information up to date to ensure team members can reach you and your timezone settings are accurate for scheduled activities.
</Tip>
