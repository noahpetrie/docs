Access Settings from the gear icon in the sidebar. Settings are organized into personal, organization, and integration categories.

## Personal settings

### Profile

Configure your personal information:

- First and last name
- Email address
- Avatar/profile photo
- Job role and bio
- Phone number and LinkedIn

### Preferences

Set your display preferences:

- **Timezone** - Your local timezone
- **Date format** - MM/DD/YYYY or DD/MM/YYYY
- **Time format** - 12-hour or 24-hour
- **Week start** - Sunday or Monday
- **Language** - English or French

### Appearance

Customize the interface:

- Theme (light, dark, system)
- Accent color
- Display density

## Organization settings

<Note>
  Organization settings require admin role.
</Note>

### General

Configure organization-level settings:

- Organization name
- Billing email
- Default settings for new projects

### Team management

Manage your team:

- View all members
- Invite new members
- Assign roles (admin, member)
- Remove members

### Billing

Manage subscription:

- Current plan
- Usage statistics
- Upgrade/downgrade
- Payment methods
- Invoice history

## Navigation

Settings are organized into sections accessible from the sidebar:

- **Profile** - Personal information
- **Appearance** - Theme and colors
- **Organization** - Company settings (admin)
- **Team** - Member management (admin)
- **Billing** - Subscription and payments (admin)
- **Integrations** - External connections
- **Communication** - Notification preferences
- **AskLeap Usage** - AI quota tracking
