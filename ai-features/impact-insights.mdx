Impact insights provide AI-generated analysis for individual change impacts, helping you understand risks, identify mitigation strategies, and plan stakeholder engagement.

## Accessing insights

1. Open a change impact detail page
2. Look for the **Insights** panel on the right side
3. Click **Generate Insights** if not already loaded

Insights are generated on-demand and cached for quick access.

## Available insights

### Risk analysis

AI assesses potential risks:

- **Risk factors** - What could go wrong
- **Likelihood** - How probable each risk is
- **Impact severity** - Consequences if the risk occurs
- **Early warning signs** - Indicators to watch

### Mitigation suggestions

Recommended actions to address the impact:

- **Quick wins** - Low-effort, high-value actions
- **Strategic initiatives** - Larger efforts with significant benefit
- **Contingency plans** - Backup approaches if primary plans fail

### Stakeholder recommendations

Guidance on engaging affected stakeholders:

- **Key influencers** - Who to prioritize
- **Engagement approach** - How to communicate
- **Potential advocates** - Who might champion the change
- **Resistance points** - Who might need extra attention

### Communication approaches

Suggested messaging strategies:

- **Key messages** - Core points to communicate
- **Timing recommendations** - When to communicate
- **Channel suggestions** - How to reach stakeholders
- **Objection handling** - Responses to common concerns

## Using insights

### As starting points

Insights are suggestions based on AI analysis. Use them as:

- Conversation starters with your team
- Checklists to ensure nothing is missed
- Inspiration for your own strategies

### Refining insights

If an insight isn't quite right:

1. Click **Regenerate** for a fresh analysis
2. Provide additional context in the impact description
3. Update stakeholder assignments for better recommendations

### Copying to mitigation plan

Convert insights to action:

1. Review the mitigation suggestions
2. Click **Add to Mitigation Plan**
3. Suggestions become mitigation items you can track

## Insight quality

Better input produces better insights:

| Factor | Impact on quality |
|--------|-------------------|
| Detailed description | Higher |
| Clear current/future states | Higher |
| Assigned stakeholders | Higher |
| Impact categories set | Moderate |
| Related impacts linked | Moderate |

### Improving insights

1. Add more detail to the impact description
2. Clearly articulate current and future states
3. Assign all affected stakeholders
4. Set appropriate categories and severity
5. Regenerate insights after updates

## Privacy

Impact insights processing:

- Uses your impact data to generate recommendations
- Does not share data with other organizations
- Does not use your data to train models
- Results are cached within your organization only
