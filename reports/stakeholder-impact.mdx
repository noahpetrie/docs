title: Stakeholder impact analysis
description: Understand readiness and engagement across your stakeholder population
---

The Stakeholder Impact Analysis report provides insights into stakeholder readiness, engagement, and distribution to help you prioritize change activities.

## Report sections

### Readiness distribution

Breakdown of stakeholders by readiness level:

| Level | Color | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| Champion | Green | Actively supporting |
| Ready | Blue | Prepared for change |
| Aware | Yellow | Understands but needs support |
| Resistant | Red | Needs intervention |

Track the shift in distribution over time.

### Influence matrix

Plot stakeholders on influence vs. support:

- **High influence, Low support** - Key targets for engagement
- **High influence, High support** - Champions to leverage
- **Low influence, Low support** - Monitor but lower priority
- **Low influence, High support** - Potential advocates

### Engagement scores

Distribution of engagement scores (0-100):

- Identify low-engagement segments
- Track improvements after interventions
- Compare across departments or groups

### Segmentation

Break down by:

- **Department** - Which areas need attention
- **Group** - Readiness by stakeholder group
- **Project** - Comparison across initiatives
- **Location** - Regional differences

## Insights and actions

### At-risk stakeholders

The report highlights:

- Resistant stakeholders with high influence
- Stakeholders with declining readiness
- Groups with low engagement scores

### Recommended actions

Based on the data, consider:

| Finding | Action |
|---------|--------|
| High-influence resistors | Schedule one-on-one discussions |
| Low engagement department | Increase communication frequency |
| Declining readiness | Investigate recent changes |
| Champion concentration | Deploy champions to resistant areas |

## Filtering

Focus the analysis:

- **Project** - Specific change initiative
- **Date range** - Point-in-time or trend analysis
- **Department** - Organizational focus
- **Group** - Specific stakeholder segments

## Export options

- **Executive summary** - One-page overview
- **Detailed report** - Full breakdown with lists
- **Raw data** - CSV for custom analysis
- **Presentation slides** - Ready for leadership meetings
