Customize your Leap Change profile and personal preferences.

## Accessing profile settings

1. Click your avatar in the bottom-left corner
2. Select **Settings**
3. Navigate to **Profile**

## Personal information

### Basic details

- **First name** and **Last name**: How you appear to team members
- **Email**: Your login email (cannot be changed here)
- **Avatar**: Profile picture shown throughout the app

### Uploading an avatar

1. Click your current avatar or the placeholder
2. Select an image file (JPG, PNG, up to 5MB)
3. Image is cropped to a square and uploaded

### Professional information

- **Job role**: Your position or title
- **Company**: Organization name
- **Bio**: Brief description about yourself
- **LinkedIn**: Link to your LinkedIn profile

### Contact details

- **Phone number**: Contact phone with country code
- **Country**: Your location

## Preferences

### Time and date

- **Timezone**: Your local timezone for scheduling and timestamps
- **Time format**: 12-hour or 24-hour clock

### Language

- **Preferred locale**: Language preference for the interface

## Appearance

Customize how Leap Change looks:

### Theme

- **Light mode**: Default light background
- **Dark mode**: Dark background for reduced eye strain
- **System**: Match your operating system preference

### Accent color

Choose a color theme that suits your preference.

## Saving changes

Changes save automatically as you update each field. A confirmation appears when changes are saved successfully.

## Password and security

To change your password:

1. Click **Change Password**
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter and confirm your new password
4. Click **Update**

<Note>
  If you signed up via Microsoft 365, password management is handled through your Microsoft account.
</Note>
