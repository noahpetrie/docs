Keep your Leap Change account secure by managing your password and authentication settings.

## Changing your password

Update your password regularly for better security:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**
2. Click **Change password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password twice
5. Click **Update password**

### Password requirements

Your password must:

- Be at least 8 characters long
- Include a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
- Contain at least one number
- Not be a commonly used password

## Forgot password

If you can't sign in:

1. Go to the sign-in page
2. Click **Forgot password?**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for the reset link
5. Click the link and set a new password

<Note>
  Password reset links expire after 24 hours. If your link has expired, request a new one.
</Note>

## Email address

Your email is used for:

- Signing in
- Password recovery
- Notifications
- Survey invitations sent to you

### Updating your email

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**
2. Click **Change email**
3. Enter your new email address
4. Verify the new email by clicking the confirmation link
5. Your email is updated once verified

## Session management

### Active sessions

View your active sessions:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. See all devices and browsers where you're signed in
3. Note the location and last active time

### Sign out of sessions

To sign out of a specific session:

1. Find the session in the list
2. Click **Sign out**
3. That device is immediately disconnected

To sign out of all sessions except your current one:

1. Click **Sign out all other sessions**
2. Confirm the action

<Tip>
  Use this if you think someone else may have access to your account.
</Tip>

## Account deletion

If you need to delete your account:

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your personal data will be removed.
</Warning>

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**
2. Scroll to **Danger zone**
3. Click **Delete account**
4. Enter your password to confirm
5. Your account is scheduled for deletion

### What happens when you delete your account

- Your personal profile is removed
- You're removed from all organizations
- Data you created (impacts, surveys, etc.) remains but shows as created by "Deleted User"
- Any organizations where you're the sole admin cannot be deleted this way

## Security best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use a strong, unique password">
    Don't reuse passwords from other services. Consider using a password manager.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Keep your email secure">
    Your email is your recovery method. Ensure your email account is secure.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sign out on shared devices">
    Always sign out when using public or shared computers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review active sessions regularly">
    Check for unfamiliar sessions that might indicate unauthorized access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Report suspicious activity">
    Contact support if you notice any unusual activity on your account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
