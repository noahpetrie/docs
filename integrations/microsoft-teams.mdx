---
title: Microsoft Teams
description: Send messages and surveys to stakeholders via Microsoft Teams
---

Connect Leap Change to Microsoft Teams to send messages, distribute surveys, and monitor channels for change-related discussions.

## Connecting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access integrations">
    Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** and find Microsoft Teams.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Click **Connect** and sign in with your Microsoft account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the bot">
    Authorize the Leap Change bot in your Teams tenant.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure channels">
    Optionally select default notification channels.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sending messages

### Direct messages

Send 1:1 messages to stakeholders:

1. Open a stakeholder profile
2. Click **Send Teams message**
3. Compose your message with personalization tokens
4. Click **Send**

### Personalization tokens

Use these tokens in your messages:

| Token | Replaced with |
|-------|---------------|
| `{{name}}` | Stakeholder's full name |
| `{{role}}` | Job title |
| `{{department}}` | Department name |
| `{{business_unit}}` | Business unit |
| `{{location}}` | Office location |
| `{{country}}` | Country |
| `{{email}}` | Email address |

### Bulk messaging

Send to multiple stakeholders:

1. Select stakeholders using checkboxes
2. Click **Send message** in the bulk actions footer
3. Choose **Microsoft Teams**
4. Compose and send

## Survey distribution

Send surveys via Teams adaptive cards:

1. Create a survey in **Surveys**
2. Click **Send survey**
3. Select stakeholders
4. Choose **Microsoft Teams** as the delivery channel
5. Send

Stakeholders receive an interactive card they can respond to directly in Teams.

## Teams bot

The Leap Change bot enables:

- **Proactive messaging**: Send messages to users who've interacted with the bot
- **Adaptive cards**: Rich interactive content for surveys
- **Conversation tracking**: Bot maintains conversation references for follow-up

<Note>
  Users must have interacted with the Leap Change bot at least once before you can send them proactive messages. Consider having users install the bot as part of onboarding.
</Note>

## Channel monitoring

Monitor Teams channels for change-related discussions:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** → **Teams**
2. Select channels to monitor
3. Configure keywords to watch

When monitored keywords appear, Leap Change can:

- Create notifications
- Suggest new change impacts
- Track discussion sentiment

## Message logging

All Teams messages sent through Leap Change are logged:

- View in stakeholder communication history
- Track delivery status
- Audit messaging activity

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="User not receiving messages">
    The user must have interacted with the Leap Change bot first. Have them search for "Leap Change" in Teams and send any message to establish the conversation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bot not found">
    The Leap Change bot may not be installed in your tenant. Contact your Teams admin to approve the bot.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Permission errors">
    Verify the integration has the required permissions. You may need to re-authorize.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
