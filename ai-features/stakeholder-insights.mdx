---
title: Stakeholder insights
description: AI-powered analysis of your stakeholder population
---

Leap Change's AI analyzes your stakeholder data to surface patterns, risks, and recommendations for more effective engagement.

## Available insights

### Key patterns

AI identifies patterns across your stakeholders:

- Department-level readiness trends
- Geographic distribution of resistance
- Influence concentration areas
- Engagement score correlations

### Relationship analysis

Understand how stakeholders connect:

- Cross-department dependencies
- Manager-report readiness alignment
- Communication network gaps
- Influence chains

### Risk identification

Surface potential risks:

- High-influence resistors
- Departments with low readiness
- Communication coverage gaps
- Stakeholders without recent contact

### Engagement recommendations

Get actionable suggestions:

- Priority stakeholders to contact
- Recommended communication approaches
- Champion candidates to leverage
- Groups needing additional support

### Executive summary

Generate leadership-ready summaries:

- Overall readiness assessment
- Key risks and mitigations
- Recommended next actions
- Progress since last period

## Accessing insights

### From stakeholder list

1. Go to **Stakeholders**
2. Click **AI Insights** button
3. View generated analysis
4. Export or share as needed

### From project view

1. Open a project
2. Go to **Stakeholders** tab
3. Click **Analyze Stakeholders**
4. See project-specific insights

## Insight types

### Population analysis

Analyzes all stakeholders to identify:

- Overall readiness distribution
- Departmental patterns
- Engagement trends
- Risk concentrations

### Segment analysis

Focus on specific groups:

1. Filter stakeholders
2. Request insights on selection
3. Compare segments
4. Identify segment-specific strategies

### Trend analysis

Track changes over time:

- Readiness improvements
- Engagement trajectory
- Survey response trends
- Risk evolution

## Using insights effectively

### Prioritize outreach

Insights help you focus on:

- Stakeholders most at risk
- High-influence individuals
- Gaps in your coverage
- Champions to activate

### Tailor communications

Use insights to:

- Segment audiences appropriately
- Address specific concerns
- Choose right channels
- Time communications optimally

### Report to leadership

Generate executive summaries:

- Quantitative readiness metrics
- Key risks and mitigations
- Progress indicators
- Resource recommendations

## Caching

Insights are cached for performance:

- Cache refreshes when data changes significantly
- Force refresh by clicking **Regenerate**
- Cached results don't consume additional quota

## Usage

Stakeholder insights use your AI quota:

- One request per analysis
- Larger populations may use more quota
- Track in **Settings** → **AskLeap Usage**
