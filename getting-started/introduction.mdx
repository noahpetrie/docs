Leap Change is an enterprise change management platform that helps organizations navigate complex transformations with confidence. Built with AI-powered insights, it centralizes change impacts, stakeholder management, and readiness tracking in one unified platform.

## What is Leap Change?

Leap Change is designed for change management professionals, project managers, and organizational leaders who need to:

- **Identify and track change impacts** across your organization
- **Manage stakeholders** with detailed engagement tracking
- **Assess readiness** through customizable surveys
- **Plan communications** that reach the right people at the right time
- **Leverage AI** to extract insights and accelerate change planning

## Core concepts

Leap Change is built around these key concepts:

### Projects

Projects are the primary organizational unit for your change initiatives. Each project contains stakeholders, change impacts, surveys, tasks, and communication plans.

### Stakeholders

Stakeholders are the people affected by or involved in your change initiatives. Track their engagement, influence levels, readiness, and communication preferences.

### Change impacts

Change impacts describe specific changes and their effects on your organization. Track severity, mitigation plans, and affected stakeholders.

### Surveys

Collect feedback and assess readiness through customizable surveys sent to stakeholders.

### Communication plans

Plan and track communications to ensure stakeholders receive the right information at the right time.
