Organization settings let admins configure company-wide options, manage team members, and control access to Leap Change.

## Accessing organization settings

1. Click your avatar in the top navigation
2. Select **Settings**
3. Click **Organization** in the sidebar

<Note>
  Organization settings require admin privileges. Contact your organization admin if you need access.
</Note>

## Organization details

### Basic information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Organization name** | Your company or team name |
| **Domain** | Your organization's web domain |

### Plan information

View your current subscription:

- **Plan tier** - Basic, Pro, or Enterprise
- **Status** - Active, Trial, or Suspended
- **Billing cycle** - Monthly or Annual

## Team management

### Viewing members

1. Go to **Settings** → **Organization** → **Team**
2. See all organization members
3. Filter by role or status

### Member details

Each member entry shows:

- Name and email
- Role (Admin or Member)
- Join date
- Last active

### Inviting new members

1. Click **Invite Member**
2. Enter their email address
3. Select their role
4. Click **Send Invitation**

Invitees receive an email with a link to join.

### Pending invitations

View and manage outstanding invitations:

- See invitation status
- Resend invitations
- Cancel invitations

### Changing member roles

1. Find the member in the team list
2. Click their current role
3. Select the new role
4. Confirm the change

| Role | Capabilities |
|------|--------------|
| **Admin** | Full access, manage settings and team |
| **Member** | Access to projects they're added to |

### Removing members

1. Find the member in the team list
2. Click **Remove**
3. Confirm the removal

Removed members:

- Lose access immediately
- Cannot be restored (must be re-invited)
- Their created content remains

## Multi-tenant access

Users can belong to multiple organizations:

### Switching organizations

1. Click your avatar
2. Select **Switch Organization**
3. Choose the target organization

### Setting primary organization

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
2. Find **Primary Organization**
3. Select your preferred default

## Organization data

### Exporting data

Request a full export of your organization's data:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Organization**
2. Click **Export Data**
3. Select what to include
4. Receive download link via email

### Data retention

Organization data is retained according to your plan:

| Plan | Retention |
|------|-----------|
| Basic | 1 year after deletion |
| Pro | 2 years after deletion |
| Enterprise | Custom |

## Danger zone

Critical organization actions:

### Transferring ownership

Transfer primary admin to another member:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Organization** → **Danger Zone**
2. Click **Transfer Ownership**
3. Select the new owner
4. Confirm with your password

### Deleting the organization

<Warning>
  Deleting an organization permanently removes all data including projects, stakeholders, and history. This cannot be undone.
</Warning>

1. Go to **Danger Zone**
2. Click **Delete Organization**
3. Type the organization name to confirm
4. Enter your password
5. Click **Delete Forever**
