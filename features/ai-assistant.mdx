Leap Change includes an AI assistant called **AskLeap** that helps you analyze change impacts, identify risks, and get intelligent recommendations.

## Accessing the AI assistant

1. Click **AskLeap** in the sidebar
2. Start a conversation with the assistant

## Available capabilities

### Change impact analysis

<Check>
  **Available now**
</Check>

Get AI-powered help with identifying and assessing change impacts:

- Describe your change initiative and get suggested impacts
- Analyze existing impacts for completeness
- Identify gaps in your impact assessment
- Get severity recommendations based on best practices

**Example prompts:**

- "We're implementing a new CRM system. What change impacts should we consider?"
- "Review my change impacts and suggest any I might have missed"
- "How severe is the impact of changing our approval workflow?"

## Using the chat interface

### Starting a conversation

1. Type your question or request in the chat input
2. Press Enter or click Send
3. The assistant will respond with analysis or suggestions

### Context awareness

The assistant has access to your:

- Change initiatives and projects
- Stakeholder database
- Change impacts
- Communication plans

This enables contextual responses based on your actual data.

### Conversation history

Your conversation is maintained during the session, allowing follow-up questions and iterative analysis.

## Best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Be specific">
    The more detail you provide about your change, the better the analysis. Include scope, timeline, and affected areas.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ask follow-ups">
    Drill down into specific areas. If the assistant identifies an impact, ask for more detail about mitigation strategies.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Validate suggestions">
    AI recommendations are starting points. Apply your organizational knowledge to validate and refine suggestions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Iterate">
    Use the assistant throughout your change lifecycle, not just at the start. It can help at every phase.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Usage tracking

Monitor your AI assistant usage:

1. Go to **Settings** > **AskLeap usage**
2. View:
   - Total queries this period
   - Usage trends over time
   - Remaining capacity (if applicable)

## Privacy and security

- Conversations are processed securely
- Your organizational data is not used to train AI models
- All queries are logged for your reference
- Data remains within your organization's boundary
