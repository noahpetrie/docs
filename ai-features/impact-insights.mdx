Leap Change provides AI-powered insights on individual change impacts, helping you understand risks and plan effective mitigation strategies.

## Accessing impact insights

1. Open any change impact detail page
2. Look for the **AI Insights** panel on the right side
3. Click to expand and view available insights

## Audience summaries

Generate summaries tailored to different audiences:

### Executive summary

AI-generated brief for leadership:

- High-level impact overview
- Business implications
- Risk assessment
- Resource requirements
- Timeline considerations

Use this when reporting to executives or steering committees.

### Manager summary

Detailed overview for middle management:

- Operational impacts on their teams
- Required actions and decisions
- Timeline and milestones
- Support resources needed
- Communication recommendations

### Frontline summary

Accessible explanation for affected employees:

- What's changing in plain language
- How it affects their daily work
- Training and support available
- Timeline and key dates
- Who to contact with questions

## Mitigation suggestions

Get AI-recommended mitigation strategies:

1. Open a change impact
2. Navigate to the **Mitigation** section
3. Click **Get AI suggestions**
4. Review recommended actions

The AI considers:

- Impact severity and category
- Affected stakeholders
- Timeline constraints
- Best practices from change management frameworks

### Example mitigation suggestions

For a high-severity technology impact:

| Suggestion | Type | Priority |
|------------|------|----------|
| Conduct pilot with small user group | Testing | High |
| Create step-by-step user guides | Documentation | High |
| Schedule hands-on training sessions | Training | Medium |
| Establish super-user support network | Support | Medium |
| Plan go-live support coverage | Support | High |

## Risk analysis

AI evaluates potential risks for each impact:

- **Adoption risk** - Will stakeholders embrace the change?
- **Timeline risk** - Can mitigation complete on schedule?
- **Dependency risk** - Are there blocking dependencies?
- **Communication risk** - Is messaging adequate?

Each risk includes:

- Risk level (High, Medium, Low)
- Contributing factors
- Recommended mitigations

## Stakeholder recommendations

Get suggestions for stakeholder engagement:

- Key stakeholders requiring personal attention
- Recommended engagement approach by stakeholder type
- Communication frequency suggestions
- Potential champions to involve

## Using insights effectively

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Generate insights early">
    Run AI analysis when impacts are first created to inform your planning from the start.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Refresh as details change">
    Regenerate insights when significant impact details change to get updated recommendations.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Combine with your expertise">
    AI suggestions are starting points. Apply your organizational knowledge to refine them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Document decisions">

  </Accordion>

## Privacy and usage

- Insights are generated on-demand
- Each generation consumes one AI request from your quota
- Results are cached to avoid unnecessary regeneration
- Your data is not used to train AI models

Track usage in **Settings** → **AskLeap Usage**.
