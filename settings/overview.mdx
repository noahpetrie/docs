Access Settings from the gear icon in the sidebar. Settings are organized into personal, organization, and integration categories.

## Personal settings

### Profile

Configure your personal information:

- **First and last name** - Display name throughout the app
- **Email address** - Login and notification email
- **Avatar/profile photo** - Upload a profile picture
- **Job role** - Your position in the organization
- **Company** - Your company name
- **Phone number** - Contact number with country code
- **Country** - Your location
- **LinkedIn profile** - Professional profile link

### Time preferences

Set your display preferences:

- **Timezone** - Your local timezone for dates and times
- **Date format** - MM/DD/YYYY or DD/MM/YYYY
- **Time format** - 12-hour or 24-hour
- **Week start** - Sunday or Monday

### Language

Choose your interface language:

- English
- French

### Appearance

Customize the interface:

- **Theme** - Light, Dark, or System (follows OS setting)
- **Accent color** - Choose from Blue, Purple, Green, Orange, Red, Yellow

## Organization settings

<Note>
  Organization settings require admin role.
</Note>

### Organization details

Configure organization-level settings:

- Organization name
- Organization description
- Billing email

### Team management

Manage your team members:

- View all members and their roles
- Invite new members via email
- Assign roles (Admin or Member)
- Remove members from the organization
- Track pending invitations

[Learn more about team management](/settings/team-management)

### Billing

Manage subscription and payments:

- View current plan and usage
- Upgrade or downgrade plans
- Manage payment methods
- View invoice history

[Learn more about billing](/settings/billing)
