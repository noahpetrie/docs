## Features

### Stakeholder Analysis

AI-powered stakeholder analysis and impact assessment for better engagement.

- Map key stakeholders and their relationships
- Assess impact and influence levels
- Track engagement and sentiment

### Change Strategy

Intelligent change management strategy generation and optimization.

- AI-generated change roadmaps
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Resource allocation optimization

### Impact Tracking

Real-time impact tracking and visualization of change initiatives.

- Real-time progress monitoring
- Custom KPI dashboards
- Automated impact reports

### Smart Insights

Data-driven insights and recommendations for successful change.

- Predictive analytics
- Trend identification
- Action recommendations
