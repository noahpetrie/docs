View and manage your Leap Change subscription, payment methods, and invoices.

<Note>
  Billing settings require admin role.
</Note>

## Current plan

View your subscription details:

- **Plan name** - Basic, Pro, or Enterprise
- **Billing cycle** - Monthly or annual
- **Next billing date** - When you'll be charged
- **Amount** - Subscription cost

## Changing plans

### Upgrading

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm payment
5. Changes take effect immediately

Upgrades are prorated—you pay the difference for the remaining billing period.

### Downgrading

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select a lower tier
4. Acknowledge any feature limitations
5. Change takes effect at next billing cycle

<Warning>
  Downgrading may result in data limitations. If you exceed the new plan's limits, you'll need to reduce data before downgrading.
</Warning>

## Payment methods

### Adding a card

1. Go to **Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add Card**
3. Enter card details
4. Save

### Updating default

1. View payment methods
2. Click **Set as Default** on your preferred card
3. Future charges use this card

### Removing cards

1. Ensure another card is set as default
2. Click **Remove** on the card to delete

## Invoices

View and download past invoices:

- Invoice date
- Amount charged
- Payment status
- PDF download

Invoices are sent to the billing email configured in organization settings.

## Usage

Track what you're using:

- **Stakeholders** - Current count vs. limit
- **Projects** - Active projects vs. limit
- **AI requests** - Monthly usage vs. quota
- **Team members** - Current vs. limit
