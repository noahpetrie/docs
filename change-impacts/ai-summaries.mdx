title: AI impact summaries
description: Generate audience-specific summaries of change impacts
---

Leap Change can generate tailored summaries of change impacts for different audiences, helping you communicate effectively with executives, managers, and frontline staff.

## Audience types

### Executive summary

Strategic, high-level overview for leadership:

- Business impact and risk
- Timeline and milestones
- Resource implications
- Decision points needed
- Success metrics

### Manager summary

Operational focus for team leaders:

- Team-specific impacts
- Actions required from managers
- Communication responsibilities
- Training and support needs
- Timeline for their teams

### Frontline summary

Day-to-day focus for affected staff:

- What changes for them personally
- When changes take effect
- Where to get help
- What they need to do
- Plain language, minimal jargon

## Generating summaries

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the change impact">
    Navigate to a change impact detail page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access AI summaries">
    Click the **Generate summaries** or **AI insights** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select audience">
    Choose which audience summaries to generate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and edit">
    Review generated summaries and edit as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use in communications">
    Copy summaries into your communication activities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Summary quality

Better summaries result from complete impact data:

| Input field | Impact on summary |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Description | Core content of summary |
| Current state | Context for what's changing |
| Future state | Clear picture of end state |
| Severity | Tone and urgency |
| Affected stakeholders | Audience relevance |
| Mitigation plan | Actions and support info |

<Tip>
  Fill in as much impact detail as possible before generating summaries. The more context you provide, the better the AI output.
</Tip>

## Editing summaries

Generated summaries are editable:

1. Click on the summary text
2. Edit in place
3. Save your changes

Add organization-specific context, terminology, and details that the AI couldn't know.

## Using summaries in communications

Summaries integrate with communication planning:

- Copy to communication activity content
- Use as email body text
- Include in Teams or Slack messages
- Base talking points on summaries

## Usage tracking

Summary generation uses AI quota:

- Each audience summary counts as one request
- Generating all three counts as three requests
- Regenerating uses additional quota
