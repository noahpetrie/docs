Leap Change integrates AI capabilities to help you work faster and surface insights you might otherwise miss. These features are powered by AskLeap, the AI assistant built into the platform.

## Available AI features

### Transcript impact extraction

Upload meeting transcripts to automatically identify change impacts:

- **Supported formats**: VTT, DOCX, TXT, PDF, or paste text directly
- AI extracts potential impacts from discussions
- Review, edit, and approve suggestions
- Save hours of manual analysis

[Learn more about AI extraction](/change-impacts/ai-extraction)

### AI chat assistant

Chat with AskLeap for change management guidance:

- Ask questions about best practices
- Get recommendations for your situation
- Explore change management methodologies
- Draft communication messages
- Analyze your change data

Access the chat from **AI Features** in the sidebar.

### Impact analysis

Get AI-generated insights on individual impacts:

- **Suggested mitigations** - Recommended actions to address impacts
- **Risk analysis** - Potential risks and considerations
- **Stakeholder recommendations** - Who should be involved
- **Communication approaches** - How to communicate about the impact

### Audience summaries

Generate tailored summaries for different audiences:

- **Executives** - High-level business impact and strategic implications
- **Managers** - Team implications and required actions
- **Frontline teams** - Day-to-day changes and available support

### Stakeholder map analysis

AI-powered geographic analysis:

- Distribution insights
- Relationship mapping
- Strategy recommendations
- Risk identification

## Accessing AI features

### From the sidebar

Navigate to **AI Features** to access:

- Chat interface for AskLeap
- Transcript upload for impact extraction
- Feature documentation

### In context

AI features appear throughout the app:

- **Change Impact detail pages** - Mitigation suggestions, audience summaries
- **Stakeholder Map report** - Geographic analysis
- **Import workflows** - Transcript extraction

## Usage and quotas

AI features consume usage quota based on your plan:

| Plan | Monthly quota |
|------|---------------|
| Basic | 100 requests |
| Pro | 1,000 requests |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |

### Tracking usage

Monitor your usage in **Settings** → **AskLeap Usage**:

- **Requests used this month** - Current consumption
- **Quota remaining** - Available requests
- **Usage by feature** - Breakdown by feature type
- **Member usage** - Usage per team member
- **Historical usage** - Trends over time

### Quota exceeded

When quota is reached:

- AI features become temporarily unavailable
- Historical results and extracted data remain accessible
- Usage resets on your billing cycle
- Upgrade your plan for more capacity

## Data privacy

AI processing follows these principles:

- Your data is not used to train AI models
- Conversations are not stored beyond your session
- Processing happens on secure infrastructure
- Enterprise customers can configure their own AI endpoints

  Start with transcript extraction—it typically provides the highest time savings for change practitioners.
