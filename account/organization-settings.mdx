title: Organization settings
description: Manage your organization's settings and team
---

Organization settings let admins manage the workspace, team members, and integrations.

## Accessing organization settings

1. Click your avatar in the top right
2. Select **Settings**
3. Go to the **Organization** tab

<Note>
  Only organization admins can access these settings.
</Note>

## Organization details

### Basic information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Organization name** | Your workspace name |
| **Logo** | Organization logo displayed in the app |
| **Domain** | Your company domain |
| **Billing email** | Email for invoices and billing notifications |

### Updating organization info

1. Edit the desired fields
2. Click **Save changes**
3. Logo uploads take effect immediately

## Team management

### Viewing team members

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. See all organization members
3. View their roles and join dates

### Inviting team members

1. Click **Invite member**
2. Enter their email address
3. Select their role:
   - **Admin**: Full access including settings
   - **Member**: Standard access to features
4. Click **Send invite**

Invited users receive an email with a join link.

### Managing pending invitations

1. View pending invitations in the team list
2. **Resend**: Send another invitation email
3. **Cancel**: Revoke the invitation

### Changing member roles

1. Find the member in the team list
2. Click the role dropdown
3. Select the new role
4. Confirm the change

### Removing team members

1. Find the member to remove
2. Click **Remove**
3. Confirm the removal

<Warning>
  Removed members immediately lose access. Their created content remains in the organization.
</Warning>

## Multiple organizations

You can belong to multiple organizations:

### Switching organizations

1. Click your avatar
2. See your organizations list
3. Click to switch to another organization

### Leaving an organization

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Click **Leave organization**
3. Confirm your departure

<Note>
  You cannot leave an organization if you're the only admin. Transfer admin rights first.
</Note>

## Archiving an organization

To archive an organization:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Organization**
2. Scroll to **Archive organization**
3. Type the organization name to confirm
4. Click **Archive**

Archiving:
- Removes access for all members
- Preserves data for potential restoration
- Stops billing

## Plan and billing

### Current plan

View your subscription details:

- Plan tier (Basic, Pro, Enterprise)
- Number of seats
- Next billing date
- Monthly cost

### Managing billing

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Manage subscription**
3. Access the billing portal to:
   - Update payment method
   - Change plan
   - Download invoices
   - Update billing email
