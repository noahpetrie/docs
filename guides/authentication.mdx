Leap Change uses secure authentication to protect your data. This guide covers account creation, signing in, and managing your credentials.

## Creating an account

1. Go to [leapchange.io](https://leapchange.io)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a secure password
5. Check your email for a confirmation link
6. Click the link to verify your account

<Tip>
  Use a work email address—this helps when joining existing organizations.
</Tip>

## Password requirements

Passwords must include:

- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number

## Signing in

1. Go to [app.leapchange.io/sign-in](https://app.leapchange.io/sign-in)
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

### Staying signed in

Your session remains active as long as you're using the app. Sessions expire after extended inactivity for security.

## Forgot password

If you've forgotten your password:

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the sign-in page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Click the link and enter a new password
5. Sign in with your new password

Reset links expire after 24 hours.

## Changing your password

To change your password while signed in:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click **Update**

## Signing out

To sign out:

1. Click your avatar in the bottom-left corner
2. Click **Sign Out**

This ends your session on the current device.

## Multiple organizations

If you belong to multiple organizations:

1. Sign in with your account
2. You'll see an organization selector
3. Choose which organization to access
4. Switch organizations anytime from the organization menu

## Security best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use a unique password">
    Don't reuse passwords from other services.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Enable browser password manager">
    Let your browser securely store and autofill credentials.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sign out on shared devices">
    Always sign out when using public or shared computers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Keep your email secure">
    Your email is the recovery method—protect it accordingly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid credentials" error

- Check that Caps Lock is off
- Verify you're using the correct email
- Try the password reset flow

### Didn't receive confirmation email

- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify you entered the correct email
- Request a new confirmation email from the sign-up page

### Account locked

After multiple failed sign-in attempts, accounts are temporarily locked. Wait 15 minutes and try again, or use the password reset flow.
