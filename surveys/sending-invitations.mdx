---
title: Sending survey invitations
description: Distribute surveys to your stakeholders
---

Once your survey is ready, send it to stakeholders via email, Microsoft Teams, Slack, or shareable links.

## Email invitations

### Selecting recipients

1. Open your survey
2. Click **Send Invitations**
3. Select stakeholders:
   - **Individual selection** - Pick specific stakeholders
   - **By group** - Select entire stakeholder groups
   - **By project** - All stakeholders in a project

### Customizing the invitation

Write a personalized message:

- **Subject line** - Email subject
- **Message body** - Introduction and context
- **Survey link** - Automatically included

### Scheduling

Send immediately or schedule for later:

- **Send now** - Deliver immediately
- **Schedule** - Pick date and time

## Microsoft Teams invitations

If you have Microsoft Teams integration enabled, send surveys directly via Teams:

1. Open your survey
2. Click **Send via Teams**
3. Select recipients (individuals or groups)
4. Customize the message (optional)
5. Send

Stakeholders receive a Teams message with a direct link to complete the survey.

<Note>
  Requires [Microsoft Teams integration](/settings/integrations#microsoft-teams) to be configured.
</Note>

## Slack invitations

If you have Slack integration enabled, send surveys via Slack direct messages:

1. Open your survey
2. Click **Send via Slack**
3. Select recipients
4. Customize the message (optional)
5. Send

Stakeholders receive a Slack message with a direct link to complete the survey.

<Note>
  Requires [Slack integration](/settings/integrations#slack) to be configured.
</Note>

## Survey links

### Unique stakeholder links

Each stakeholder receives a unique link that:

- Pre-identifies them (no login required)
- Tracks their response status
- Links responses to their profile

### Anonymous links

For truly anonymous feedback:

1. Go to survey settings
2. Enable **Anonymous mode**
3. Share the generic survey URL
4. Responses won't be linked to identities

## Managing invitations

### Tracking status

View invitation status in the survey dashboard:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Sent** | Invitation delivered |
| **Opened** | Email was opened |
| **Started** | Survey was accessed |
| **Completed** | Response submitted |

### Sending reminders

For stakeholders who haven't responded:

1. Go to the survey's **Responses** tab
2. Filter by **Not completed**
3. Click **Send Reminder**
4. Customize the reminder message

<Tip>
  Wait at least 3-5 days before sending reminders. Multiple reminders within a short period can reduce response rates.
</Tip>
