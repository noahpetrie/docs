AskLeap is the AI assistant built into Leap Change. Chat with AskLeap to get guidance on change management best practices, draft communications, and explore your change data.

## Accessing the chat

1. Navigate to **AI Features** in the sidebar
2. Click **Chat with AskLeap**
3. Type your question or request

## What you can ask

### Change management guidance

- "How do I handle resistant stakeholders?"
- "What are best practices for communicating difficult changes?"
- "How should I structure a readiness assessment?"
- "What's the ADKAR model?"

### Draft communications

- "Write an announcement email for our ERP migration"
- "Create talking points for a town hall about restructuring"
- "Draft a follow-up message for stakeholders who haven't completed the survey"

### Analyze your data

- "Which stakeholders have the lowest readiness scores?"
- "What are the most common concerns in survey responses?"
- "Summarize the high-severity impacts for the Finance project"

### Get recommendations

- "What mitigation strategies should I consider for this technology change?"
- "Who should I focus on engaging first?"
- "What questions should I include in a readiness survey?"

## Chat features

### Conversation context

AskLeap remembers the conversation within your session:

- Ask follow-up questions
- Reference previous answers
- Build on earlier suggestions

### Suggested prompts

The chat interface offers suggestion chips for common questions to help you get started.

### Copy responses

Click the copy icon to copy any response for use in:

- Communication plans
- Emails
- Documentation
- Reports

## Tips for better responses

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Be specific about context">
    Instead of "How do I handle this change?", try "How do I communicate a technology change affecting 200 users in Finance who have low technology skills?"
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reference your data">
    Mention specific projects, stakeholder groups, or impacts for more relevant answers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ask for alternatives">
    If the first suggestion doesn't fit, ask "What other approaches could work?" or "Can you suggest something less formal?"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Usage and quotas

Chat interactions count toward your organization's AI quota. Track usage in **Settings** → **AskLeap Usage**.

## Privacy

- Conversations are not stored beyond your session
- Your data is not used to train AI models
- Enterprise customers can use their own AI endpoints
