The Home dashboard is your starting point after logging in. It provides a quick overview of your change management activities and shortcuts to common tasks.

## Dashboard overview

The Home page displays:

### Welcome section

Personalized greeting with time-based messages (morning, afternoon, evening) and quick access to your most relevant information.

### Key metrics

Four primary metrics at a glance:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Stakeholders** | Total stakeholders in your organization |
| **Change impacts** | Active impacts across all projects |
| **Surveys** | Open surveys collecting feedback |
| **AI tools** | Available AI-powered features |

### Quick actions

Common tasks accessible from the dashboard:

- Add a stakeholder
- Create a change impact
- Launch a survey
- Access AI features

### Onboarding checklist

For new users, a checklist guides you through essential first steps:

1. **Add a stakeholder** - Import or create your first stakeholder
2. **Record an impact** - Document a change impact
3. **Explore AI** - Try AI-powered features
4. **Generate a report** - Create your first visualization

Progress is tracked and the checklist disappears once completed.

## Navigation shortcuts

The dashboard provides quick links to:

- **Recent projects** - Your most recently accessed initiatives
- **Pending tasks** - Tasks assigned to you
- **Recent activity** - Latest changes in your organization

## Using the dashboard

### Daily workflow

Start your day on the Home dashboard to:

1. Review key metrics for changes
2. Check pending tasks
3. Identify stakeholders needing attention
4. Access priority actions

### Customization

The dashboard adapts based on your role and activity patterns, surfacing the most relevant information for your work.
