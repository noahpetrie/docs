title: AI transcript extraction
description: Automatically extract change impacts from meeting transcripts using AI
---

Leap Change uses AI to analyze meeting transcripts and automatically identify potential change impacts. This saves hours of manual review and ensures you don't miss important impacts mentioned in discussions.

## Supported formats

Upload transcripts in these formats:

- **VTT**: Video Text Tracks (common from Teams, Zoom)
- **DOCX**: Microsoft Word documents
- **TXT**: Plain text files
- **PDF**: PDF documents

## Uploading a transcript

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to import">
    Go to **Change Impacts** and click **Import** → **From Transcript**, or access via **AI Features**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your file">
    Select or drag your transcript file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select target project">
    Choose which project the extracted impacts should be added to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run extraction">
    Click **Extract** and wait for AI analysis (typically 30-60 seconds).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What gets extracted

The AI identifies both explicit and implied changes in your transcript:

### Explicit changes

Direct statements about changes:

- "We're migrating to the new CRM system"
- "The approval workflow will change"
- "Everyone needs to complete training by March"

### Implied changes

Changes suggested by context:

- Discussion of new processes or tools
- Mentions of upcoming deadlines
- References to things "going away" or "being replaced"

### For each impact, AI provides:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Title | Brief summary of the impact |
| Description | Detailed explanation |
| Category | People, Process, Technology, Security, Business |
| Severity | Assessed impact level |
| Affected roles | Who is likely affected |
| Source quote | Original text from transcript |
| Confidence | How confident the AI is in this extraction |

## Reviewing extracted impacts

Extracted impacts appear in a review queue. For each suggestion:

- **Accept**: Creates the impact with suggested details
- **Edit and accept**: Modify details before creating
- **Reject**: Discard the suggestion
- **Merge**: Combine with an existing impact if it's a duplicate

<Tip>
  Review all extracted impacts before accepting. AI suggestions are starting points that benefit from your organizational context.
</Tip>

## Duplicate detection

The AI automatically checks extracted impacts against existing impacts in your project:

- **Exact matches**: Flagged as duplicates
- **Similar impacts**: Highlighted for review
- **New impacts**: Marked as ready to add

## Confidence scores

Each extraction includes a confidence score:

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|----------|
| High (80-100%) | Clear, explicit mention of change |
| Medium (50-79%) | Likely a change, some interpretation needed |
| Low (below 50%) | Possible change, requires verification |

Focus review time on lower-confidence extractions.

## Best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use complete transcripts">
    Full meeting transcripts provide more context than excerpts or notes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Clean up transcripts">
    Remove headers, footers, and system messages for better results. Manual cleanup of obvious transcription errors improves accuracy.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Provide context in project">
    Having existing impacts in the project helps duplicate detection.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review in batches">
    Process extracted impacts while the meeting is fresh in memory.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Usage tracking

Transcript extraction consumes AI quota:

- Usage tracked in **Settings** → **AskLeap Usage**
- Larger transcripts use more quota
- Results are cached for re-review
