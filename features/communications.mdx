Effective communication is essential for change success. Leap Change helps you plan, coordinate, and track communications to keep stakeholders informed and engaged.

## Communication plans

A communication plan outlines your strategy for engaging stakeholders throughout a change initiative.

### Creating a plan

1. Navigate to **Communications** in the sidebar
2. Click **New plan**
3. Enter the plan details:
   - **Name**: Descriptive title for the plan
   - **Description**: Overview of communication objectives
   - **Initiative**: Link to the relevant project
   - **Priority**: High, medium, or low
   - **Start date**: When communications begin
   - **End date**: When the campaign concludes
4. Click **Create**

## Communication activities

Activities are individual communication actions within a plan.

### Adding activities

1. Open a communication plan
2. Click **Add activity**
3. Define the activity:
   - **Title**: Brief description
   - **Type**: Meeting, email, announcement, training, etc.
   - **Target audience**: Which stakeholders
   - **Channel**: How the message will be delivered
   - **Scheduled date**: When it will happen
   - **Owner**: Who is responsible
   - **Content**: The message or talking points
4. Click **Save**

### Activity types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Email | Direct email communication |
| Meeting | In-person or virtual meeting |
| Announcement | Broad organizational communication |
| Training | Educational session |
| Newsletter | Regular update publication |
| Town hall | Large group presentation |

### Activity status

Track progress through:

- **Draft**: Being prepared
- **Scheduled**: Ready and waiting for delivery date
- **In progress**: Currently being executed
- **Completed**: Successfully delivered
- **Canceled**: No longer needed

## Targeting stakeholders

Connect communications to specific audiences:

### Individual stakeholders

Select specific people who should receive the communication.

### Stakeholder groups

Target predefined groups for efficient bulk communications.

### By impact

Identify stakeholders affected by specific change impacts and communicate about those changes.

## Communication calendar

View all scheduled communications in calendar format:

- See activity density over time
- Identify communication gaps
- Avoid overwhelming stakeholders
- Coordinate timing across initiatives

## Email integration

Send communications directly from Leap Change:

1. Create an email activity
2. Compose your message using the editor
3. Select recipients from your stakeholder database
4. Schedule or send immediately
5. Track delivery and engagement

## Progress tracking

Monitor communication plan execution:

- **Activity count**: Total vs. completed activities
- **Completion rate**: Percentage of activities finished
- **Timeline status**: On track, at risk, or delayed
