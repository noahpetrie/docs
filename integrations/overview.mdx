## Integration benefits

### Stakeholder sync

Automatically import users from your directory services:

- Keep stakeholder data current with scheduled syncs
- Map organizational attributes (department, job title, location)
- Reduce manual data entry
- Maintain consistency across systems

### Direct communication

Reach stakeholders through their preferred channels:

- Send survey invitations via Teams or Slack
- Deliver change notifications in-platform
- Track message delivery and engagement

### Channel monitoring

Capture change-related discussions happening in your organization:

- Monitor Teams channels for relevant conversations
- Surface insights for impact analysis
- Stay informed without manual tracking

## Managing integrations

1. Navigate to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. View connected services and their status
3. Configure sync settings and permissions
4. Disconnect integrations when no longer needed

## Security and permissions

All integrations follow these principles:

- **Least privilege**: Request only necessary permissions
- **Secure storage**: OAuth tokens encrypted at rest
- **Audit trail**: Connection and sync events logged
- **Revocable**: Disconnect at any time

<Note>
  Admin permissions are required to connect or configure integrations.
</Note>
