Surveys in Leap Change help you gather stakeholder feedback and measure readiness for change. Use surveys to understand concerns, track sentiment over time, and identify who needs additional support.

## Survey types

Leap Change supports two survey types:

### Quick surveys

Single-question surveys for rapid pulse checks:

- One rating question (1-10 scale)
- Optional follow-up text field
- Fast to create and complete
- Ideal for regular check-ins

### Comprehensive surveys

Multi-section surveys for detailed assessment:

- Multiple sections with ordered questions
- Mix of rating and text questions
- Weighted scoring options
- Suitable for formal readiness assessments

## Survey lifecycle

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Draft** | Being created, not yet visible to stakeholders |
| **Open** | Actively accepting responses |
| **Closed** | No longer accepting new responses |

## Key features

### Distribution methods

- **Email invitations** - Send personalized emails with unique survey links
- **Shareable links** - Copy survey URL to share via any channel
- **Anonymous mode** - Collect feedback without identifying respondents

### Response tracking

- **Real-time updates** - See responses as they come in
- **Response status** - Track sent, opened, started, and completed states
- **Reminders** - Send follow-up invitations to non-responders
- **Preview emails** - Review invitation emails before sending

### Readiness calculation

Survey responses automatically update stakeholder readiness levels:

- Ratings 9-10 → Champion
- Ratings 7-8 → Ready  
- Ratings 5-6 → Aware
- Ratings 1-4 → Resistant
