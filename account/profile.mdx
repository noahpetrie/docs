Your profile contains your personal information, contact details, and preferences that appear throughout Leap Change.

## Accessing profile settings

1. Click your avatar in the top navigation
2. Select **Settings**
3. Click **Profile** in the sidebar

## Personal information

### Basic details

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **First name** | Your given name |
| **Last name** | Your family name |
| **Email** | Your login email (cannot be changed here) |
| **Phone** | Contact phone number |

### Professional information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Job role** | Your position or title |
| **Company** | Your organization name |
| **Bio** | Brief description about yourself |
| **LinkedIn** | Your LinkedIn profile URL |

### Location

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Country** | Your country |
| **Timezone** | Your local timezone |
| **Time format** | 12-hour or 24-hour display |

## Profile photo

### Uploading a photo

1. Click your current avatar or the camera icon
2. Select an image file (JPG, PNG, or GIF)
3. Crop the image if needed
4. Click **Save**

### Photo requirements

- Minimum size: 100x100 pixels
- Maximum size: 5 MB
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF

### Removing a photo

1. Click your avatar
2. Select **Remove Photo**
3. Confirm the removal

Your initials will display instead.

## Changing your email

Email changes require verification:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
2. Click **Change Email**
3. Enter your new email address
4. Check your new email for a verification link
5. Click the link to confirm

<Note>
  Your email is your login credential. After changing it, use the new email to sign in.
</Note>

## Password management

### Changing your password

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click **Update Password**

### Password requirements

- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number

### Forgot password

If you can't remember your password:

1. Go to the sign-in page
2. Click **Forgot Password**
3. Enter your email
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Create a new password

## Locale preferences

### Timezone

Select your timezone for accurate date and time display:

1. Find the **Timezone** dropdown
2. Search for your city or timezone
3. Select the correct option
4. Save changes

All dates and times in Leap Change will display in your selected timezone.

### Time format

Choose your preferred time format:

- **12-hour** - 2:30 PM
- **24-hour** - 14:30

## Saving changes

Changes are not saved automatically:

1. Make your edits
2. Click **Save Changes** at the bottom
3. A confirmation message appears

To discard changes, navigate away without saving or click **Cancel**.
