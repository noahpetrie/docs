Manage how Leap Change communicates with you.

## Accessing communication settings

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Select **Communication** from the settings menu

## Email notifications

Configure which emails you receive from Leap Change:

### Survey notifications

- **Survey invitations** - Receive emails when invited to complete a survey
- **Survey reminders** - Follow-up reminders for incomplete surveys

### Task notifications

- **Task assignments** - Notified when tasks are assigned to you
- **Due date reminders** - Alerts for upcoming deadlines
- **Task comments** - When someone comments on your tasks

### Activity notifications

- **Project updates** - Changes to projects you're involved in
- **Communication activities** - Upcoming communications you're part of
- **Mentions** - When someone mentions you in notes or comments

## Notification frequency

Control how often you receive notification emails:

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Immediate** | Receive emails as events happen |
| **Daily digest** | One summary email per day |
| **Weekly digest** | One summary email per week |
| **Off** | No email notifications |

## In-app notifications

Notifications also appear in the notification center (bell icon) within Leap Change:

- View recent activity
- Mark notifications as read
- Quick access to relevant items

## Unsubscribe

You can also manage email preferences from unsubscribe links in any notification email.

<Note>
  Critical account notifications (password resets, security alerts) cannot be disabled.
</Note>
