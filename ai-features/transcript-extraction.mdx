Upload meeting transcripts and let AI automatically identify potential change impacts, saving hours of manual analysis.

## Supported file formats

Leap Change accepts transcripts in these formats:

| Format | Extension | Source |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| WebVTT | .vtt | Microsoft Teams, Zoom, Google Meet |
| Word document | .docx | Manual transcription, Word exports |
| Plain text | .txt | Any text export |
| PDF | .pdf | Exported documents |

Maximum file size: 10 MB

## Extraction process

### Step 1: Upload your transcript

1. Navigate to **Change Impacts**
2. Click **Import** → **From Transcript**
3. Choose your upload method:
   - **File upload** - Drag and drop or select a file
   - **Paste text** - Copy and paste transcript content directly
4. Select the associated project

### Step 2: AI processing

Once uploaded, Leap Change's AI:

1. Parses the transcript content
2. Identifies statements about changes, impacts, and concerns
3. Extracts current and future state descriptions
4. Suggests appropriate severity levels
5. Groups related mentions together

Processing typically takes 30-60 seconds depending on transcript length.

### Step 3: Review extracted impacts

You'll see a list of AI-suggested impacts:

- **Title** - Generated summary of the impact
- **Description** - Details extracted from the discussion
- **Confidence** - AI confidence level in the extraction
- **Source quote** - The original transcript excerpt

### Step 4: Accept, edit, or reject

For each suggested impact:

| Action | When to use |
|--------|-------------|
| **Accept** | Impact is accurate and complete |
| **Edit and accept** | Needs refinement before saving |
| **Reject** | Not a valid impact or not relevant |
| **Merge** | Duplicate of an existing impact |

## Best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use high-quality transcripts">
    Automated transcription services may have errors. Review and correct obvious mistakes before uploading for better results.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Include context in meetings">
    Train your team to use phrases like "this change will affect..." or "the impact on..." during discussions to help AI identification.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Select the correct project">
    Choosing the right project helps the AI filter relevant impacts and provides better context for extraction.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review all suggestions">
    Always review AI suggestions before accepting. The AI may suggest duplicates, miss context, or misinterpret discussions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Example extraction

**Transcript excerpt:**
> "With the new CRM system, the sales team will need to enter customer data differently. Currently they use spreadsheets, but after go-live they'll need to use the contact management module. This is a significant change for the 50 sales reps."

**AI-extracted impact:**
- **Title**: Sales team adopts CRM contact management module
- **Current state**: Sales team uses spreadsheets for customer data entry
- **Future state**: Sales team uses CRM contact management module
- **Severity**: High (50 affected users, significant workflow change)
- **Category**: Process, Technology
