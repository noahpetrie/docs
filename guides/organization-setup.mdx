This guide walks you through setting up your organization in Leap Change and managing team access.

## Creating your organization

When you first sign up for Leap Change, you'll create your organization:

1. Complete the registration process
2. You'll be prompted to name your organization
3. Enter a clear, recognizable name
4. Click **Create organization**

<Note>
  Your organization name can be changed later in settings.
</Note>

## Organization settings

Access organization settings:

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Select **Organization** or navigate to **Dashboard** > **Org**

### Basic information

- **Organization name**: How your org appears throughout the platform
- **Timezone**: Default timezone for scheduling
- **Industry**: For benchmarking (optional)

## Team management

### Inviting team members

1. Go to **Dashboard** > **Org** > **Team**
2. Click **Invite member**
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role:
   - **Admin**: Full access including settings and member management
   - **Member**: Standard access to initiatives and data
5. Click **Send invite**

The invitee will receive an email with instructions to join.

### Managing existing members

From the Team page:

- View all organization members
- Change member roles
- Remove members from the organization

### Role permissions

| Action | Admin | Member |
|--------|-------|--------|
| View initiatives | ✓ | ✓ |
| Create/edit initiatives | ✓ | ✓ |
| View stakeholders | ✓ | ✓ |
| Manage stakeholders | ✓ | ✓ |
| Access reports | ✓ | ✓ |
| Invite members | ✓ | ✗ |
| Remove members | ✓ | ✗ |
| Organization settings | ✓ | ✗ |
| Billing management | ✓ | ✗ |

## Account settings

Manage your personal account:

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar

### Profile

- Update your name
- Change your avatar
- Set your job role
- Update contact information

### Appearance

- Toggle between light and dark themes
- Set your preferred color scheme

### Security

- Change your password
- Enable two-factor authentication (if available)

## Billing

Manage your subscription:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. View current plan
3. Upgrade or change plans
4. Update payment methods
5. View billing history

<Info>
  Only organization admins can access billing settings.
</Info>

## Data and privacy

### Data ownership

- All data belongs to your organization
- Data is isolated from other organizations
- You can export your data at any time

### Security measures

- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Role-based access control
- Audit logging for sensitive actions
- Regular security assessments
