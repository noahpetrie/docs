AskLeap is Leap Change's AI-powered chat assistant that helps you with change management questions, strategy recommendations, and content creation.

## Accessing the chat assistant

1. Navigate to **AI Features** in the sidebar
2. Click **Chat** or select **AskLeap**
3. Start a conversation

You can also access AskLeap from contextual prompts throughout the app.

## What AskLeap can help with

### Change management guidance

Ask questions about best practices:

- "What's the best approach for managing resistance to change?"
- "How should I structure a stakeholder communication plan?"
- "What are the key success factors for technology adoption?"

### Strategy recommendations

Get tailored advice based on your situation:

- "How should I engage executives who are skeptical of this change?"
- "What mitigation strategies work for high-impact process changes?"
- "How can I improve stakeholder readiness scores?"

### Content drafting

Generate communication content:

- "Draft an announcement email for our new system launch"
- "Create talking points for a town hall about the reorganization"
- "Write FAQ responses for common employee concerns"

### Data analysis

Ask questions about your change data:

- "Which stakeholders have the lowest readiness scores?"
- "What are the most common impact categories in my project?"
- "How many surveys have been completed this month?"

## Using the chat interface

### Starting a conversation

1. Type your question in the chat input
2. Press Enter or click Send
3. AskLeap responds with guidance

### Follow-up questions

Continue the conversation naturally:

- Ask clarifying questions
- Request more detail on specific points
- Explore related topics

Each conversation maintains context, so AskLeap remembers what you've discussed.

### Starting fresh

Click **New Chat** to start a new conversation without previous context.

## Best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Be specific">
    Instead of "Help with stakeholders," ask "How should I engage the finance department stakeholders who are resistant to the new expense system?"
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Provide context">
    Share relevant details: "We're implementing a new CRM. The sales team has used the old system for 10 years. How should we approach training?"
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Iterate on responses">
    If the first response isn't quite right, ask for adjustments: "Make that more concise" or "Focus more on the risk mitigation aspect."
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review and customize">
    AskLeap provides suggestions—always review and adapt them to your specific situation and organizational culture.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Limitations

AskLeap is designed to assist, not replace, change management expertise:

- Responses are suggestions, not definitive answers
- Always validate recommendations against your organizational context
- For sensitive communications, have stakeholders review before sending
- AskLeap doesn't have access to real-time external information

## Privacy

Your conversations with AskLeap:

- Are not used to train AI models
- Are not stored beyond your session
- Are processed on secure infrastructure
- Are isolated to your organization
