This guide walks you through setting up your first change initiative in Leap Change.

## Prerequisites

- A Leap Change account (sign up at [app.leapchange.io](https://app.leapchange.io))
- Basic information about your change initiative

## Step 1: Create your organization

When you first sign up, you'll be prompted to create your organization:

1. Enter your organization name
2. Invite team members (optional)
3. Click **Create organization**

<Note>
  You can invite additional team members later from the organization settings.
</Note>

## Step 2: Create a program (optional)

Programs help you group related initiatives:

1. Navigate to **Programs** in the sidebar
2. Click **New program**
3. Enter a name and description
4. Click **Create**

## Step 3: Create your first initiative

1. Go to the **Dashboard**
2. Click **New initiative**
3. Fill in the initiative details:
   - **Name**: A clear, descriptive title
   - **Description**: Overview of what the initiative involves
   - **Status**: Current phase (planning, active, completed)
   - **Program**: Optionally link to a program
4. Click **Create initiative**

## Step 4: Add stakeholders

Identify the people affected by or involved in your change:

1. Go to **Stakeholders** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add stakeholder**
3. Enter stakeholder details:
   - Name and contact information
   - Role and department
   - Influence level (high/medium/low)
   - Support level (supporter/neutral/resistant)
4. Click **Save**

<Tip>
  You can import stakeholders in bulk using CSV upload.
</Tip>

## Step 5: Log change impacts

Document how the change will affect your organization:

1. Navigate to **Change impacts**
2. Click **Add impact**
3. Describe the impact:
   - **Title**: Brief summary
   - **Category**: People, process, technology, etc.
   - **Severity**: Critical, high, medium, low
   - **Current state**: How things work today
   - **Future state**: How things will work after the change
   - **Affected stakeholders**: Who is impacted
4. Click **Save**

## Step 6: Plan communications

Create a communication plan for your stakeholders:

1. Go to **Communications**
2. Click **New plan**
3. Define your communication activities:
   - Target audience
   - Message content
   - Delivery channel
   - Schedule
4. Click **Create plan**
