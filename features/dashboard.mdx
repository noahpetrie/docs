The dashboard is your starting point in Leap Change, providing an overview of your change management activities and quick access to common actions.

## Dashboard overview

When you sign in, you're greeted with a personalized dashboard that includes:

- **Welcome message** with time-based greeting
- **Quick stats** showing key metrics at a glance
- **Quick actions** to jump to common tasks
- **Getting started checklist** for new users

## Quick stats

See your organization's change management metrics:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Stakeholders** | Total stakeholders in your organization |
| **Change impacts** | Active impacts being managed |
| **Surveys** | Surveys created and sent |
| **AI tools** | AI feature usage |

Click any stat to navigate to that section.

## Quick actions

Access common tasks with one click:

- **Stakeholder management** - View and manage stakeholders
- **Change impacts** - Track and update impacts
- **Reports** - Access analytics and visualizations
- **AI assistant** - Get intelligent recommendations
- **Surveys** - Create and manage surveys
- **Team** - Manage organization members

## Getting started checklist

New users see an onboarding checklist to help you get up and running:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your first stakeholder">
    Create your initial stakeholder entry to start building your database.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record your first impact">
    Document a change impact to begin tracking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore AI features">
    Try AskLeap to see AI-powered capabilities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate your first report">
    Create a report to visualize your data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Track your progress as you complete each step. The checklist disappears once all items are complete.

## Organization dashboard

Admins can access an organization-level dashboard at **Dashboard** > **Org**:

- **Team overview** - See all organization members
- **Usage statistics** - Track platform usage
- **Subscription status** - View current plan details
- **Recent activity** - See recent actions across the organization

## Customization

The dashboard adapts to your usage:

- Recently accessed projects appear for quick navigation
- Suggested actions based on your work patterns
- Notifications for items requiring attention
