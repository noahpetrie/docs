## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Leap Change?">
    Leap Change is an AI-powered change management platform that helps organizations navigate complex transformations. It centralizes stakeholder management, change impact tracking, surveys, and communications in one unified platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who is Leap Change for?">
    Leap Change is designed for:
    - Change management professionals
    - Project managers leading organizational changes
    - HR and transformation teams
    - Consultants managing client change initiatives
    - Leaders overseeing organizational transformations
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I try Leap Change before subscribing?">
    Yes, new accounts include a trial period with access to all features. Contact us for enterprise trial arrangements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data secure?">
    Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use row-level security to ensure complete data isolation between organizations. See our security page for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account and access

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I invite team members?">
    Go to **Settings** → **Organization** → **Team** and click **Invite member**. Enter their email address and select their role (admin or member).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between admin and member roles?">
    **Admins** can manage team members, billing, integrations, and all organization settings. **Members** can view and edit stakeholders, impacts, surveys, and other data but cannot access admin settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I belong to multiple organizations?">
    Currently, each account is associated with one organization. For multiple organizations, use separate email addresses.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I transfer ownership of my organization?">
    Contact support to transfer organization ownership. The new owner must be an existing admin.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Projects and programs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between a project and a program?">
    A **project** (also called initiative) is a single change effort with its own stakeholders, impacts, and communications. A **program** groups multiple related projects for coordinated management and reporting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I move stakeholders between projects?">
    Yes. Stakeholders can be assigned to multiple projects. Edit the stakeholder profile or use bulk operations to add/remove project assignments.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I archive a project?">
    Archived projects are hidden from default views but all data is preserved. You can restore archived projects at any time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I delete a project?">
    Yes, but deletion is permanent. All associated data (impacts, surveys, tasks) will be removed. Consider archiving instead if you might need the data later.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Stakeholders

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many stakeholders can I add?">
    Stakeholder limits depend on your plan. Check **Settings** → **Billing** to see your current usage and limits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I import stakeholders from a spreadsheet?">
    Yes. Go to **Stakeholders** → **Import** and upload a CSV file. Download the template to see the required format.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I sync stakeholders from Microsoft 365?">
    Enable the Microsoft 365 integration in **Settings** → **Integrations**. Configure sync settings to automatically import users from your Azure AD/Entra ID.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to stakeholder data if I delete them?">
    Deleted stakeholders are permanently removed from all projects and surveys. Survey responses and historical data referencing them will show "Deleted Stakeholder."
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Surveys

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are survey responses anonymous?">
    You can choose. When sending surveys, responses can be tracked to identify who responded, but the content can still be anonymous. Enable full anonymous mode for completely unidentified responses.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can stakeholders edit their survey responses?">
    Once submitted, responses cannot be edited by the stakeholder. They would need to contact you to submit a new response.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I remind stakeholders to complete a survey?">
    In the survey's **Responses** tab, filter to see non-responders and click **Send Reminder** to re-invite them.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export survey responses?">
    Yes. Go to the survey results and click **Export** to download responses as CSV or PDF.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## AI features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What AI features are available?">
    Current AI features include: transcript impact extraction (upload meeting transcripts to automatically identify change impacts), chat-based assistance with AskLeap, and impact analysis suggestions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data used to train AI models?">
    No. Your organizational data is not used to train AI models. Conversations are processed securely and not retained beyond your session.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What counts toward my AI quota?">
    Each AI request (transcript extraction, chat message, impact analysis) counts toward your monthly quota. Check usage in **Settings** → **AskLeap Usage**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What file formats does transcript extraction support?">
    VTT (from Teams/Zoom), DOCX, TXT, and PDF files are supported for AI impact extraction.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing and plans

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does billing work?">
    Plans are billed monthly or annually. Annual plans offer a discount. Changes to your plan take effect on your next billing cycle (downgrades) or immediately (upgrades, prorated).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel my subscription?">
    Yes. Go to **Settings** → **Billing** and cancel your subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel?">
    Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation. After that, it is permanently deleted. Export your data before canceling if you need to keep it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer discounts for non-profits?">
    Yes. Contact us with proof of non-profit status to discuss available discounts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
