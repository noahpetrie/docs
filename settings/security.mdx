Monitor and manage your account security in Leap Change.

## Accessing security settings

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Select **Security** from the settings menu

## Active sessions

View all devices and browsers where you're currently signed in.

### Session information

Each session displays:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Device type** | Desktop or mobile |
| **Browser** | Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc. |
| **Operating system** | Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android |
| **IP address** | Network location |
| **Last seen** | When the session was last active |

### Current session

Your active session is marked with a green "Current session" badge.

### Managing sessions

#### Revoke other sessions

To sign out of all devices except your current session:

1. Click **Revoke all** next to "Other sessions"
2. Confirm the action
3. All other sessions are immediately terminated

<Warning>
  Revoking sessions signs you out of those devices. You'll need to sign in again on those devices.
</Warning>

## Security best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use a strong password">
    Choose a unique password with at least 12 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review sessions regularly">
    Periodically check your active sessions and revoke any you don't recognize.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sign out from shared devices">
    Always sign out when using shared or public computers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Keep your email secure">
    Your email is your recovery method. Ensure your email account is secure.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Suspicious activity

If you see sessions you don't recognize:

1. Click **Revoke all** to terminate all other sessions
2. Change your password immediately
3. Check your email for any unauthorized password reset requests
4. Contact support if you suspect unauthorized access
