AskLeap is the AI chat assistant built into Leap Change. Ask questions, get recommendations, and receive guidance on change management best practices.

## Accessing the chat

1. Navigate to **AI Features** in the sidebar
2. Select **Chat**
3. Start typing your question

Alternatively, use the keyboard shortcut `Cmd+K` (Mac) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows) to open quick chat.

## What you can ask

### Change management guidance

- "What's the best approach for communicating this change?"
- "How should I handle resistance from key stakeholders?"
- "What are common pitfalls in system migrations?"

### Best practices

- "What does ADKAR recommend for this situation?"
- "How often should I send stakeholder communications?"
- "What metrics should I track for change adoption?"

### Content assistance

- "Draft an announcement email for our new process"
- "Create survey questions to assess readiness"
- "Suggest mitigation strategies for this impact"

### Analysis help

- "What risks should I consider for this initiative?"
- "Which stakeholders need the most attention?"
- "How can I improve survey response rates?"

## Using context

AskLeap works better with context. When asking questions:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="With context">
    "We're implementing a new CRM system that affects 500 sales reps across 3 regions. They currently use spreadsheets and email. What's the best communication strategy?"
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Without context">
    "What's the best communication strategy?"
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

The more specific your question, the more relevant the response.

## Chat history

- Conversations are saved within your session
- Start a new conversation with **New Chat**
- Previous messages provide context for follow-ups
- History is cleared when you close the browser

## Tips for better responses

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Be specific">
    Include details about your situation, stakeholders, and constraints.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ask follow-ups">
    If the first response isn't quite right, ask clarifying questions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Request formats">
    Ask for lists, tables, or step-by-step instructions if that's what you need.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Iterate on content">
    Ask the AI to revise drafts: "Make this more formal" or "Shorten this."
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Limitations

- AskLeap doesn't have access to real-time data from your projects
- Responses are general guidance, not specific to your data
- Always verify recommendations against your organizational context
- Complex or sensitive decisions should involve human judgment

## Usage tracking

Chat interactions consume AI credits:

- Each message exchange uses 1 credit
- Complex queries may use additional credits
- Monitor usage in **Settings** → **AskLeap Usage**
