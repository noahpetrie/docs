Protect your Leap Change account by monitoring sessions and managing security settings.

## Active sessions

View and manage all devices and browsers where you're signed in.

### Viewing sessions

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. See the list of active sessions including:
   - **Browser** - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.
   - **Operating system** - Windows, macOS, iOS, Android
   - **IP address** - Connection location
   - **Last seen** - When the session was last active

### Identifying your current session

Your current session is marked with a badge so you can identify which sessions are from other devices.

### Revoking sessions

To sign out of other devices:

1. Find the session you want to end
2. Click **Revoke**
3. Confirm the action

The session is immediately terminated. The user on that device will need to sign in again.

### Revoking all sessions

To sign out everywhere except your current session:

1. Click **Revoke all other sessions**
2. Confirm the action
3. All other sessions are immediately terminated

Use this if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.

## Password management

### Changing your password

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **Change password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click **Update password**

Password requirements:

- Minimum 8 characters
- Mix of letters and numbers recommended
- Avoid common passwords

### Password reset

If you forget your password:

1. Click **Forgot password?** on the sign-in page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for the reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password

Reset links expire after 24 hours.

## Session timeout

Sessions remain active for extended periods for convenience, but you can:

- Sign out manually when done
- Use **Revoke all sessions** periodically
- Sign out from the profile menu

## Security best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use strong passwords">
    Choose passwords that are at least 12 characters and combine letters, numbers, and symbols.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Don't share credentials">
    Each user should have their own account. Never share login credentials.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Monitor sessions regularly">
    Check active sessions periodically and revoke any you don't recognize.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sign out on shared devices">
    Always sign out when using shared or public computers.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account access issues

If you're locked out of your account:

1. Try password reset via email
2. Check spam folder for reset emails
3. Contact your organization admin
4. Reach out to Leap Change support

## Audit logging

<Note>
  Available on Enterprise plans
</Note>

Enterprise organizations can access audit logs showing:

- Sign-in attempts (successful and failed)
- Session creation and termination
- Settings changes
- Administrative actions

Contact your admin or Leap Change support to access audit logs.
