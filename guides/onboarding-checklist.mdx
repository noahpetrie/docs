Welcome to Leap Change! Follow this checklist to set up your organization and start managing change effectively.

## Account setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Go to **Settings** → **Profile** and add:
    - Your name and photo
    - Job role and contact information
    - Timezone and preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your organization">
    Update the default organization name in **Settings** → **Organization**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite team members">
    Add colleagues from **Settings** → **Team**. Assign admin or member roles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect integrations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Microsoft 365 (optional)">
    Connect to sync stakeholders from Entra ID automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Microsoft Teams (optional)">
    Enable Teams messaging for stakeholder communications.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Slack (optional)">
    Connect Slack for notifications and messaging.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create your first project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new project">
    Go to **Projects** → **New project** and complete the wizard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add stakeholders">
    Import via CSV or add manually. Include influence and support levels.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Document change impacts">
    Create your first impact with current state, future state, and severity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Explore key features

<Steps>
  <Step title="Try AI extraction">
    Upload a meeting transcript to automatically identify change impacts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a survey">
    Send a quick pulse check to stakeholders.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a report">
    View the stakeholder map or impact report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use AskLeap">
    Ask the AI assistant a question about your change initiative.
  </Step>
</Steps>
