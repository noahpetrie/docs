Leap Change provides AI-generated insights throughout the platform to help you make better decisions and save time on analysis.

## Impact insights

On change impact detail pages, the AI Insights panel provides:

### Risk analysis

- Potential risks associated with the impact
- Likelihood and severity assessment
- Contributing factors

### Mitigation suggestions

- Recommended actions to address the impact
- Best practices for similar changes
- Sequencing recommendations

### Stakeholder recommendations

- Who to involve in mitigation planning
- Potential champions to leverage
- Groups requiring additional attention

### Communication guidance

- Key messages for this change
- Timing considerations
- Channel recommendations

## Accessing impact insights

1. Open any change impact
2. Look for the **AI Insights** panel on the detail page
3. Click **Generate Insights** if not already loaded
4. Review the analysis and recommendations

## Stakeholder insights

AI analysis is available on stakeholder profiles:

### Engagement analysis

- Patterns in their survey responses
- Communication engagement trends
- Predicted readiness trajectory

### Personalized recommendations

- Suggested engagement approaches
- Communication style preferences
- Potential concerns to address

## Bulk insights

For analysis across multiple items:

### Impact summary

1. Select multiple impacts in the table
2. Click **AI Summary**
3. Get an overview of common themes and priorities

### Stakeholder analysis

1. Filter stakeholders (e.g., by department)
2. Click **Analyze Group**
3. Receive aggregated insights and recommendations

## Insight quality

AI insights are:

- Generated based on your actual data
- Informed by change management best practices
- Updated as your data changes

<Note>
  AI insights are recommendations, not guarantees. Always apply your professional judgment and organizational knowledge.
</Note>

## Regenerating insights

To get fresh analysis:

1. Click **Regenerate** on the insights panel
2. New insights are generated based on current data
3. Previous insights are replaced

## Usage tracking

Insight generation counts toward your AI quota. Monitor usage in **Settings** → **AskLeap Usage**.
