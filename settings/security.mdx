View and manage your active sessions across devices.

## Accessing security settings

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Select **Security** from the settings menu

## Active sessions

See all devices and browsers currently signed into your account.

### Session information

Each session displays:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Device type** | Desktop or mobile device icon |
| **Browser & OS** | Browser name and operating system |
| **IP address** | Network address of the session |
| **Last seen** | How recently the session was active |

### Current session

Your current session is marked with a green badge. This is the browser and device you're using right now.

### Other sessions

View all other active sessions. These could be:

- Other browsers on the same computer
- Mobile devices
- Other computers you've logged into

## Managing sessions

### Revoke all other sessions

To sign out of all other devices:

1. Click **Revoke all sessions**
2. Confirm the action
3. All sessions except your current one are terminated

Use this if:

- You suspect unauthorized access
- You've lost a device
- You want to ensure you're only logged in on one device

<Warning>
  Revoking sessions will immediately sign out those devices. Users will need to sign in again.
</Warning>

## Security best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use a strong password">
    Choose a unique password that you don't use for other services. Consider using a password manager.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review sessions regularly">
    Check your active sessions periodically to ensure you recognize all devices.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sign out on shared devices">
    Always sign out when using shared or public computers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Keep your email secure">
    Your email is used for password recovery. Ensure your email account is secure.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
