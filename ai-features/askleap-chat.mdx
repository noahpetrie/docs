AskLeap is your AI assistant for change management, available to help with analysis, recommendations, and content generation.

## Accessing AskLeap

1. Click the **AskLeap** icon in the navigation
2. Or use the keyboard shortcut `Cmd/Ctrl + K` and type "AskLeap"
3. The chat interface opens in a panel

## Capabilities

### Change impact analysis

Ask AskLeap to analyze your change impacts:

- Summarize impacts by severity or category
- Identify patterns across impacts
- Suggest mitigation strategies
- Highlight stakeholder concerns

**Example prompts:**
- "Summarize the high-severity impacts for Project X"
- "What are the common themes across our change impacts?"
- "Suggest mitigations for our technology-related impacts"

### Stakeholder insights

Get help understanding your stakeholder landscape:

- Identify engagement gaps
- Analyze readiness levels
- Suggest communication approaches
- Highlight at-risk groups

**Example prompts:**
- "Which stakeholder groups have low readiness?"
- "How should I engage resistant stakeholders?"
- "What communication methods work best for executives?"

### Content generation

Generate change management content:

- Communication drafts
- Survey questions
- Mitigation plans
- Executive summaries

**Example prompts:**
- "Draft an announcement for the new CRM system"
- "Create survey questions about training readiness"
- "Write an executive summary of our change program"

### Best practices

Get guidance on change management approaches:

- Methodology recommendations
- Timing suggestions
- Risk mitigation strategies
- Success metrics

**Example prompts:**
- "What's the best approach for rolling out this change?"
- "How should I handle resistance to this technology change?"
- "What metrics should I track for this initiative?"

## Using context

AskLeap has access to your Leap Change data:

- Projects and programs
- Stakeholders and groups
- Change impacts
- Survey results

Reference specific items in your prompts:
- "Analyze the impacts in the Digital Transformation program"
- "Which stakeholders in the Sales team haven't responded to surveys?"

## Conversation history

AskLeap maintains conversation context:

- Ask follow-up questions
- Reference previous answers
- Build on earlier analysis

<Tip>
  Start new conversations for unrelated topics to keep context focused.
</Tip>
