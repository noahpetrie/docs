Leap Change's AI can suggest mitigation strategies for your change impacts based on best practices and your specific situation.

## Requesting suggestions

1. Open a change impact
2. Navigate to the **Mitigation** section
3. Click **Suggest mitigations** or **Get AI suggestions**
4. Review generated recommendations

## What AI considers

Suggestions are based on:

| Factor | Influence on suggestions |
|--------|-------------------------|
| Impact description | Type of actions needed |
| Severity level | Urgency and depth of response |
| Category | Domain-specific strategies |
| Current state | What needs to change |
| Future state | Target outcomes |
| Affected stakeholders | Who needs support |
| Existing mitigations | Gaps to fill |

## Suggestion types

AI generates suggestions across these areas:

### Communication actions

- Stakeholder briefings
- FAQ documents
- Town halls and Q&A sessions
- Regular update cadence

### Training actions

- Skills gap analysis
- Training program design
- Learning materials
- Hands-on practice opportunities

### Support actions

- Help desk preparation
- Peer champion networks
- Escalation paths
- Resource availability

### Process actions

- Pilot programs
- Phased rollouts
- Fallback procedures
- Success criteria

## Using suggestions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review suggestions">
    Read through all AI-generated recommendations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select relevant ones">
    Choose suggestions that fit your context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to mitigation plan">
    Click to add selected suggestions as mitigation items.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize">
    Edit items to add specifics like owners and deadlines.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Mitigation item tracking

Added mitigations become trackable items:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Not started | Mitigation identified but work not begun |
| In progress | Actively being implemented |
| Completed | Mitigation fully in place |
