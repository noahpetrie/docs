The Communication Preferences report helps you tailor your communication strategy based on stakeholder preferences.

## Report sections

### Preference distribution

Pie chart showing stakeholders by preferred communication method:

- Email
- In-person meetings
- Video calls
- Phone calls
- Instant messaging
- Group presentations

Use this to prioritize communication channels.

### Department breakdown

Compare preferences across departments:

- Identify departmental trends
- Plan targeted communications
- Allocate resources appropriately

### Engagement by channel

Analyze which channels drive engagement:

- Response rates by channel
- Survey completion by invitation method
- Activity participation rates

## Using the report

### Planning communications

Based on preferences:

1. Choose primary channels for each stakeholder segment
2. Plan backup channels for important messages
3. Avoid over-relying on unpopular channels

### Optimizing outreach

| Finding | Action |
|---------|--------|
| High email preference | Prioritize email campaigns |
| Low meeting attendance | Shift to async communications |
| Mixed preferences | Offer multiple channel options |

## Data requirements

Communication preferences are captured:

- During stakeholder creation
- Via survey questions
- Through engagement tracking

Encourage stakeholders to set their preferences for better data.

## Export
