title: AI impact extraction
description: Automatically extract change impacts from meeting transcripts
---

Leap Change uses AI to analyze meeting transcripts and automatically identify potential change impacts. This saves time and ensures you don't miss important impacts mentioned in discussions.

## Supported formats

Upload transcripts in these formats:

- **VTT** - Web Video Text Tracks (from Teams, Zoom, etc.)
- **DOCX** - Word documents
- **TXT** - Plain text files
- **PDF** - PDF documents

You can also paste transcript text directly without uploading a file.

## Extraction process

### Step 1: Upload or paste transcript

1. Go to **Change Impacts**
2. Click **Import** → **From Transcript**
3. Either:
   - Upload a transcript file
   - Paste transcript text directly
4. Choose the associated project

### Step 2: AI analysis

Leap Change processes your transcript and:

- Identifies statements about changes
- Extracts current and future state descriptions
- Suggests severity levels based on context
- Groups related mentions together
- Determines relevant categories

This typically takes 30-60 seconds depending on transcript length.

### Step 3: Review suggestions

You'll see a list of suggested impacts with:

- **Title** - AI-generated title
- **Description** - Extracted details
- **Current state** - How things work today
- **Future state** - Proposed changes
- **Severity** - Suggested impact level
- **Confidence** - How confident the AI is in the extraction
- **Source quote** - The transcript excerpt that triggered the suggestion

### Step 4: Accept or reject

For each suggestion:

- **Accept** - Creates the impact with the suggested details
- **Edit and accept** - Modify details before creating
- **Reject** - Discard the suggestion
- **Merge** - Combine with an existing impact

<Tip>
  Review all suggestions before accepting. AI may suggest duplicates or miss context that you can correct.
</Tip>

## Tips for better extraction

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use clear meeting transcripts">
    Automated transcription works best when speakers are clear. Manual cleanup of obvious transcription errors improves results.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Include context in discussions">
    Mentioning phrases like "this change will affect..." or "the impact on..." helps the AI identify impacts more accurately.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review all suggestions">
    AI may suggest duplicates or miss context—always review before accepting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Provide project context">
    Selecting the correct project helps the AI filter relevant impacts and suggests appropriate stakeholders.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Process longer transcripts">
    Longer, detailed transcripts typically yield more accurate results than brief meeting notes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

AI extraction uses your organization's AI quota. Track usage in **Settings** → **AskLeap Usage**.

Each transcript analysis counts as one or more AI requests depending on length.
