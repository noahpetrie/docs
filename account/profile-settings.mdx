---
title: Profile settings
description: Manage your personal profile and preferences
---

Customize your Leap Change profile and set your personal preferences.

## Accessing profile settings

1. Click your avatar in the top right
2. Select **Settings**
3. Go to the **Profile** tab

## Profile information

### Personal details

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **First name** | Your given name |
| **Last name** | Your family name |
| **Job role** | Your position or title |
| **Company** | Your organization name |
| **Bio** | Brief description about yourself |

### Contact information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Phone** | Contact number with country code |
| **LinkedIn** | Link to your LinkedIn profile |
| **Country** | Your location |

### Profile photo

Upload a profile picture:

1. Click the avatar area
2. Select an image file (PNG, JPEG, or GIF)
3. Crop if needed
4. Click **Save**

Supported formats: PNG, JPEG, GIF (max 10MB)

## Regional preferences

### Timezone

Set your timezone for accurate date and time display:

1. Click the **Timezone** dropdown
2. Search for your city or timezone
3. Select from the list
4. Save changes

### Date and time formats

| Setting | Options |
|---------|----------|
| **Time format** | 12-hour (AM/PM) or 24-hour |
| **Date format** | MM/DD/YYYY, DD/MM/YYYY, or YYYY-MM-DD |
| **Week starts on** | Sunday or Monday |

### Language

Select your preferred display language:

- English
- French

The interface updates immediately after saving.

## Saving changes

1. Make your updates
2. Click **Save changes**
3. A confirmation appears when saved

<Note>
  Some changes (like timezone) affect how dates appear throughout the application.
</Note>

## Password management

### Changing your password

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
2. Click **Change password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click **Update password**

### Password requirements

- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number

## Account deletion

To delete your account:

1. Contact support
2. Request account deletion
3. Confirm via email

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed.
</Warning>
