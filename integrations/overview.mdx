## Integration benefits

### Stakeholder sync

Keep your stakeholder database current by syncing with your identity provider:

- Import users from Microsoft Entra ID
- Sync group memberships automatically
- Update stakeholder details on schedule
- Deactivate departed employees

### Multichannel communications

Reach stakeholders through their preferred channels:

- **Email**: Direct email communications
- **Microsoft Teams**: Bot messages and adaptive cards
- **Slack**: Direct messages and channel posts

### Survey distribution

Send surveys through multiple channels:

- Email invitations with unique links
- Teams messages with embedded survey cards
- Slack Block Kit surveys

## Connecting integrations

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. Select the integration to connect
3. Authorize with admin credentials
4. Configure sync and messaging settings

<Note>
  Integration configuration requires admin role. Contact your organization admin if you don't see the integrations settings.
</Note>

## Security

All integrations follow security best practices:

- OAuth 2.0 authentication
- Minimal permission scopes
- Encrypted data transfer
- Tokens stored securely
- Admin consent where required
