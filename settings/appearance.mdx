Personalize your Leap Change experience with theme and color preferences.

## Theme

Choose your preferred color scheme:

| Theme | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Light** | Bright background with dark text |
| **Dark** | Dark background with light text |
| **System** | Automatically matches your device settings |

### Setting your theme

1. Go to **Settings** → **Appearance**
2. Click on your preferred theme option
3. The change applies immediately

A preview of each theme shows how the interface will look.

## Accent color

Customize the primary accent color used throughout the interface:

- **Blue** - Default professional look
- **Purple** - Creative, modern feel
- **Green** - Nature-inspired, calming
- **Orange** - Energetic, warm
- **Red** - Bold, attention-grabbing
- **Yellow** - Bright, optimistic

The accent color affects:

- Primary buttons
- Links and highlights
- Progress indicators
- Selected items
- Focus states

### Changing accent color

1. Go to **Settings** → **Appearance**
2. Click on a color in the accent color picker
3. The color updates across the application

## Display preferences

Additional display settings:

### Reduced motion

If you prefer less animation:

1. Enable **Reduce motion** in appearance settings
2. Animations and transitions will be minimized

### Compact mode

For information-dense displays:

1. Enable **Compact mode**
2. Reduces spacing and padding throughout the interface
3. Shows more content on screen

## Settings sync

Your appearance preferences are saved to your account and apply across all devices where you use Leap Change.
