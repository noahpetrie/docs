## What is Leap Change?

Leap Change is an AI-powered change management platform designed to help organizations navigate complex transformations. It provides a comprehensive suite of tools for stakeholder analysis, change impact assessment, communication planning, and progress tracking.

## Key capabilities

### Stakeholder management

Maintain a comprehensive database of stakeholders with:
- Role, influence, and support level tracking
- Power-interest grid visualization
- Stakeholder grouping and categorization
- Communication preference management
- Engagement scoring

### Change impact tracking

Systematically identify and manage change impacts:
- Log impacts with severity and status
- Categorize by impact type (process, technology, people)
- Track mitigation plans and progress
- Link impacts to affected stakeholders

### Communication planning

Orchestrate stakeholder communications:
- Create communication plans with activities
- Schedule and track message delivery
- Monitor engagement and response rates

### Surveys and feedback

Gather stakeholder input:
- Create customizable surveys
- Track response rates
- Analyze sentiment and trends

### AI assistant

Leverage AI for smarter change management:
- Automated change impact analysis
- Risk prediction and recommendations
- Communication strategy suggestions

## Who uses Leap Change?

Leap Change is built for:

- **Change managers** leading transformation initiatives
- **Project managers** overseeing complex projects
- **HR leaders** managing organizational change
- **Consultants** advising on change strategies
