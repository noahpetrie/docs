Personalize the visual appearance of Leap Change to match your preferences.

## Accessing appearance settings

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Select **Appearance** from the settings menu

## Theme

Choose how Leap Change displays:

### Light mode

Bright interface with white backgrounds. Ideal for well-lit environments.

### Dark mode

Dark interface with reduced brightness. Easier on the eyes in low-light conditions and may reduce battery usage on OLED displays.

### System

Automatically matches your operating system's appearance setting. Switches between light and dark mode based on your device preferences.

## Accent color

Customize the primary color used for buttons, links, and highlights:

| Color | Use case |
|-------|----------|
| **Blue** | Default, professional appearance |
| **Purple** | Creative, distinctive look |
| **Green** | Nature-inspired, calm aesthetic |
| **Orange** | Energetic, attention-grabbing |
| **Red** | Bold, high-contrast option |
| **Yellow** | Bright, optimistic feel |

Click any color swatch to apply it immediately. The accent color affects:

- Primary buttons
- Active navigation items
- Links and interactive elements
- Focus indicators

## Preview

The settings page shows a preview of each theme option before you select it, helping you visualize how the interface will look.

## Persistence

Your appearance settings are saved to your profile and apply across all devices where you sign in.
