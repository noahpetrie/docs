Leap Change's AI analyzes your change impacts and suggests mitigation strategies to help stakeholders prepare for and adopt changes successfully.

## How it works

When you request mitigation suggestions:

1. AI analyzes the impact details
2. Considers affected stakeholders
3. Evaluates severity and category
4. Generates contextual recommendations

## Types of suggestions

### Action items

Specific tasks to address the impact:

- Communication activities
- Training requirements
- Support mechanisms
- Process changes

### Communication approaches

Recommendations for stakeholder messaging:

- Key messages to deliver
- Timing considerations
- Channel recommendations
- Audience-specific variations

### Risk mitigations

Strategies to reduce impact risks:

- Resistance management tactics
- Escalation paths
- Contingency plans
- Success indicators

## Accessing suggestions

### From impact detail

1. Open a change impact
2. Scroll to **Mitigation** section
3. Click **Get AI Suggestions**
4. Review recommendations
5. Add useful suggestions to your plan

### Bulk suggestions

1. Go to **Change Impacts** list
2. Select multiple impacts
3. Click **Bulk Actions** → **Get Mitigation Suggestions**
4. Review suggestions for each impact

## Understanding suggestions

Each suggestion includes:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Action** | What to do |
| **Rationale** | Why this helps |
| **Owner suggestion** | Who should be responsible |
| **Timeline** | When to complete |
| **Priority** | Urgency level |

## Adding suggestions to plans

### Accept and add

1. Review the suggestion
2. Click **Add to Plan**
3. Suggestion becomes a mitigation item
4. Assign owner and due date

### Modify and add

1. Click **Edit** on the suggestion
2. Modify as needed
3. Click **Add to Plan**

### Reject

1. Click **Dismiss**
2. Suggestion is removed
3. Doesn't affect future suggestions

## Audience-specific summaries

Generate impact summaries tailored to different audiences:

### Executive summary

- High-level business impact
- Resource implications
- Risk assessment
- Decision points

### Manager summary

- Team-level impacts
- Support needed from managers
- Timeline and milestones
- Escalation guidance

### Frontline summary

- Day-to-day changes
- Training available
- Support resources
- What they need to do

### Generating summaries

1. Open a change impact
2. Click **Generate Summaries**
3. Select audience types
4. Review and edit as needed
5. Use in communications
