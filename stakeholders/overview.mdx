Stakeholders are the people affected by or involved in your change initiatives. Effective stakeholder management is critical to change success.

## Stakeholder data model

Leap Change captures comprehensive stakeholder information:

### Basic information

- **Name** - First and last name
- **Email** - Contact email address
- **Phone** - Phone number with country code
- **Department** - Business department
- **Business unit** - Organizational business unit
- **Job role** - Position or title
- **Location** - Office location
- **Country** - Geographic location

### Engagement metrics

- **Influence level** - Low, Medium, or High - how much power they have
- **Support level** - Supportive, Neutral, or Resistant - their stance on the change
- **Engagement score** - 0-100 calculated from interactions and activities
- **Readiness level** - Champion, Ready, Aware, or Resistant

### Communication

- **Preferred communication method** - Email, Teams, Slack, etc.
- **Last contact date** - Most recent communication
- **Key interests** - Topics they care about
- **Concerns** - Known issues or resistance factors

## Stakeholder views

Access stakeholders from the **Stakeholders** section in the sidebar.

### Table view

The default view shows all stakeholders in a sortable, filterable table. You can:

- **Sort** by any column (click column header)
- **Filter** by department, influence, readiness, project, location, country
- **Search** by name or email
- **Customize columns** - Show/hide columns and resize widths
- **Save views** - Create personal or shared views with preset filters

### Stakeholder profile

Click any stakeholder to see their detailed profile:

- **Overview** - Contact info, role, and engagement metrics
- **Activity timeline** - All interactions including surveys, communications, meetings, and Teams messages
- **Survey responses** - Historical feedback with ratings and comments
- **Associated projects** - Which initiatives they're linked to
- **Change impacts** - Impacts that affect this stakeholder

### Stakeholder groups

Organize stakeholders by navigating to **Stakeholders** → **Groups** tab:

- Create logical groupings (by team, location, or custom criteria)
- View group membership counts
- Apply bulk actions to group members
