Leap Change's AI can analyze your stakeholder data to surface patterns, relationships, and strategic recommendations.

## Available insights

### Pattern analysis

Identify key patterns across your stakeholder database:

- Department concentrations
- Influence level distribution
- Support level trends
- Geographic clusters

### Relationship analysis

Understand organizational connections:

- Department and business unit relationships
- Manager hierarchies
- Cross-functional groupings
- Communication network patterns

### Strategy recommendations

Get actionable engagement strategies:

- High-priority stakeholders to focus on
- Engagement approach by segment
- Communication channel recommendations
- Timing suggestions for outreach

### Risk identification

Surface potential risks:

- Stakeholders likely to resist
- Underengaged key influencers
- Communication gaps
- Change burden concentrations

### Summary generation

Create executive summaries:

- Overall stakeholder landscape
- Key statistics and trends
- Recommended actions
- Risk highlights

## Accessing insights

1. Navigate to **Stakeholders** or **AI Features**
2. Click **Analyze stakeholders** or **Get insights**
3. Select which analysis types to run
4. Review generated insights

## Using insights

Insights inform your change strategy:

- **Pattern analysis**: Understand who's affected and how
- **Relationship analysis**: Identify influence networks
- **Strategy recommendations**: Focus your engagement efforts
- **Risk identification**: Proactively address potential issues

<Tip>
  Run stakeholder analysis early in your project to shape your engagement strategy, then repeat periodically to track changes.
</Tip>

## Data requirements

Better insights come from complete stakeholder data:

- Name, department, role
- Influence and support levels
- Communication preferences
- Project associations
- Group memberships
