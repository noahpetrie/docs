Keep your Leap Change account secure with password management and session controls.

## Changing your password

To update your password:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password
5. Confirm the new password
6. Click **Update**

### Password requirements

Your password must:

- Be at least 8 characters long
- Include a mix of letters and numbers
- Not be a commonly used password

## Session management

View and manage your active login sessions.

### Viewing active sessions

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security** → **Sessions**
2. See all devices where you're logged in:
   - Device type and browser
   - Location (approximate)
   - Last active time
   - Current session indicator

### Signing out of sessions

To sign out of a specific session:

1. Find the session in the list
2. Click **Sign Out**
3. That device is immediately logged out

To sign out of all sessions except your current one:

1. Click **Sign Out All Other Sessions**
2. Confirm the action
3. All other devices are immediately logged out

<Tip>
  If you notice unfamiliar sessions, sign out of them immediately and change your password.
</Tip>

## Multi-organization access

If you belong to multiple organizations in Leap Change:

### Switching organizations

1. Click your organization name in the sidebar
2. Select a different organization from the list
3. You're switched to that organization's workspace

### Organization-specific data

Each organization has completely separate data:

- Stakeholders
- Projects and programs
- Change impacts
- Surveys and responses
- Team members

Your personal settings (profile, preferences) apply across all organizations.

## Account deletion

To delete your Leap Change account:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**
2. Scroll to **Danger Zone**
3. Click **Delete Account**
4. Confirm by entering your password
5. Your account and personal data are permanently deleted

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you're the only admin of an organization, you must transfer ownership first.
</Warning>
