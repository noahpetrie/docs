Integrate with Microsoft Teams to reach stakeholders directly in their collaboration workspace.

## Features

- Send survey invitations via Teams messages
- Post change notifications to channels
- Track message delivery status
- Monitor team channels for feedback

## Prerequisites

- Microsoft 365 integration connected
- Teams license for your organization
- Channel access permissions

## Setting up Teams

### Connect to Teams

1. Ensure Microsoft 365 is connected first
2. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
3. Find **Microsoft Teams** and click **Configure**
4. Select the teams and channels to use
5. Save your configuration

### Select channels

Choose which channels to enable for notifications:

1. Browse available teams
2. Expand a team to see channels
3. Select channels for notifications
4. Configure channel purposes (surveys, updates, etc.)

## Sending messages

### Survey invitations

Send surveys directly to stakeholders via Teams:

1. Create or open a survey
2. Click **Send** → **Teams**
3. Select recipients
4. Customize the message (optional)
5. Send the invitation

Stakeholders receive a card with:
- Survey title and description
- Direct link to respond
- Due date (if set)

### Channel notifications

Post updates to team channels:

1. Navigate to the item to share
2. Click **Share** → **Teams**
3. Select the target channel
4. Add a message (optional)
5. Post to the channel

## Channel monitoring

Monitor Teams channels for stakeholder feedback:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** → **Teams**
2. Click **Channel monitoring**
3. Select channels to monitor
4. Configure monitoring settings

Monitored messages appear in your notification inbox.

## Message tracking

Track delivery and engagement:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Sent** | Message delivered to Teams |
| **Read** | Recipient viewed the message |
| **Clicked** | Recipient clicked the survey link |

## Teams bot

Leap Change includes a Teams bot for direct interactions:

- Responds to direct messages
- Provides quick access to surveys
- Shares status updates

### Installing the bot

1. Download the Leap Change app package
2. Upload to your Teams admin center
3. Approve for your organization
4. Users can add it from the app catalog

## Troubleshooting

### Messages not sending

- Verify Teams integration is connected
- Check channel permissions
- Ensure bot is installed in the team

### Recipients not receiving messages

- Confirm stakeholders have Teams licenses
- Check that email addresses match
- Verify stakeholder Teams IDs are synced

### Bot not responding

- Ensure bot is installed in the team
- Check bot permissions
- Verify webhook subscriptions are active
