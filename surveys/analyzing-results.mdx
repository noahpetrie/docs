---
title: Analyzing survey results
description: Understand and act on survey responses
---

Leap Change provides real-time analytics on survey responses to help you understand stakeholder sentiment and readiness.

## Results dashboard

Access results from any survey:

1. Go to **Surveys**
2. Click on a survey
3. Select the **Results** tab

### Key metrics

- **Response rate** - Percentage of invitees who responded
- **Average rating** - Mean score across rating questions
- **Completion rate** - Started vs. completed surveys

### Response breakdown

View responses by:

- **Question** - See ratings for each question
- **Stakeholder** - Individual response details
- **Time** - Response trends over time
- **Segment** - Break down by department, group, or other attributes

## Rating analysis

For rating questions (1-10 scale):

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Average** | Mean score |
| **Distribution** | How responses spread across the scale |
| **Detractors** | Ratings 1-6 (indicating concerns) |
| **Passives** | Ratings 7-8 (neutral) |
| **Promoters** | Ratings 9-10 (positive) |

## Text response analysis

Open-ended responses are displayed with:

- **Word cloud** - Common themes highlighted
- **Full responses** - Read individual answers
- **Sentiment indicators** - Positive, negative, neutral tagging

## Filtering results

Slice data by stakeholder attributes:

- Department
- Location
- Influence level
- Stakeholder group
- Project assignment

This helps identify which segments need attention.

## Exporting data

Export results for external analysis:

1. Click **Export** in the results view
2. Choose format (CSV or PDF)
3. Select what to include:
   - Summary statistics
   - Individual responses
   - Charts and visualizations

## Acting on results

Based on survey insights:

1. **Identify at-risk groups** - Low scores indicate need for intervention
2. **Address common concerns** - Text responses reveal specific issues
3. **Update readiness levels** - Scores inform stakeholder readiness
4. **Plan targeted communications** - Reach groups with specific concerns

<Tip>
  Share summarized results with stakeholders to demonstrate you're listening and taking action on their feedback.
</Tip>
