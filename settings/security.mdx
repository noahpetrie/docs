Security settings help you manage active login sessions and protect your account.

## Active sessions

View and manage all devices and browsers where you're currently logged in.

### Viewing sessions

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. See a list of all active sessions

Each session shows:

- **Device type** - Desktop or mobile
- **Browser and OS** - Chrome on macOS, Safari on iOS, etc.
- **IP address** - Where the session originated
- **Last seen** - When the session was last active

Your current session is marked with a green badge.

### Session information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Device | Type of device (desktop, mobile, tablet) |
| Browser | Browser name (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) |
| Operating system | Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android |
| IP address | Network address of the session |
| Last activity | When the session was last used |

### Revoking sessions

If you see a session you don't recognize or want to log out from other devices:

1. Find the session you want to revoke
2. Click **Revoke** to end that session
3. The user on that device will be logged out

To log out from all other devices at once:

1. Click **Revoke all other sessions**
2. All sessions except your current one will be terminated

<Warning>
  If you revoke your current session, you will be logged out immediately.
</Warning>

## Security best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use strong passwords">
    Choose a unique password with at least 12 characters, mixing letters, numbers, and symbols.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review sessions regularly">
    Check your active sessions periodically to ensure no unauthorized access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Log out on shared devices">
    Always log out when using a shared or public computer.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Revoke suspicious sessions">
    If you see a session from an unfamiliar location or device, revoke it immediately and change your password.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Password management

### Changing your password

1. Go to the sign-in page
2. Click **Forgot password**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for the reset link
5. Set a new password

Password resets are sent to your registered email address for security.

### Password requirements

Your password must:

- Be at least 8 characters long
- Not be a commonly used password
- Not match your email address
